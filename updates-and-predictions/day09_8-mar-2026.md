     - *Ground escalation* (unlikely in March): Kurdish offensive expands; US special forces enter Iran. Abrahamian's 500k/draft scenario. Structural pressure builds but March too early.

4. **Beyond March: The long tail.**
   - Fertiliser damage to 2026 harvests is already being locked in, regardless of when hostilities end.
   - Gulf as AI/financial hub damaged; investor reallocation to Singapore, India.
   - Iran's dependence on China increases (Chatham House); US influence in region diminishes.
   - The war's economic and political costs outlast the war itself (Ron Paul, Ritter).
