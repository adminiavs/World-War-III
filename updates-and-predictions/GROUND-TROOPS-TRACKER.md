## Why this is a critical escalation (plain language)

**From the air, only buildings can be destroyed.** The war so far has been bombs, missiles, and drones. No Americans have entered Iran. That means the war stays distant for most Americans.

**Ground troops change everything.** Once American soldiers are on Iranian soil:
- Casualties will rise rapidly and be impossible to hide
- The war becomes personal -- every soldier is someone's family member
- Iran, and its proxies, will target them by every means available
- Political support at home will collapse (74% already oppose ground troops)
- The November 2026 midterms become a referendum on the war

**The Strait of Hormuz problem.** The US cannot currently reopen the Strait from the air alone. Iran's IRGC uses swarm tactics -- small missile boats, drones, naval mines. The only way to physically secure the Strait may be to seize Iranian territory (Kharg Island, coastal positions). That requires Marines.

**The nuclear problem.** Rubio said it: the US needs to physically retrieve or destroy Iran's enriched uranium stockpiles. Air strikes alone cannot do this -- the IAEA confirmed that after June 2025. Someone has to go in.

**The political trap.** If Trump sends ground troops, he faces a Vietnam-style political disaster. If he doesn't, he cannot achieve his stated objectives (stopping nuclear capability, reopening Hormuz). There may be no good option.
