## tl;dr

Chomsky lecture on US–Iran relations. Intro: "public intellectual" label declined. US–Iran relations begin 1952 when Britain called in US to overthrow Iranian parliamentary regime; radical asymmetry, Melian dialogue (strong do what they wish, weak suffer). Geostrategic background: pre–WWII US richest but Britain/France ruled; oil economy drove US expansion; Venezuela (Wilson took from Britain, ran until 1999 Chávez); 2002 coup US-supported, overturned; 1920s Red Line, 1930s Saudi, US–Britain contention; WWII planners: Middle East oil "stupendous strategic power," "greatest material prize"; Eisenhower axiom; Kennan veto power over other industrial economies; Brézinski Iraq = critical leverage; control not consumption. Post–WWII: France dismissed, Britain displaced; British Foreign Office on US "economic imperialism... benign avuncular." Iran: nationalization, 1953 coup, Shah; NYT "object lesson... fanatical nationalism"; 40% to US firms (firms resisted, Eisenhower forced – global vs parochial); parallel today (corporations want Iran market, state punishes); Cuba successful defiance. 1967: Israel destroyed Nasser secular nationalism, US favoured radical Islam; Israel–Iran–Saudi pillars; Nixon Doctrine cops on the beat; 1948 JCS Israel base; Palestinians "human dust." 1979: Mossad chief coup idea; Carter Huyser; Iraq invaded 1980; Israel armed Iran during war; US backed Saddam (Rumsfeld 1983), Vincennes 290, Reagan blamed Iran for Saddam chemical warfare; USS Stark; sanctions; 1989 Iraqi nuclear engineers invited, Dole delegation; Saddam invaded Kuwait → "evil"; Bush feared Saddam withdrawal with puppet; Resolution 687; sanctions genocidal. Iran sanctions 2010, Trump 2018; livestream to Iran barred; cyber war; "all options open" vs UN Charter 2.4. Gallup greatest threat (US won, question dropped); US intel Iran low spending, defensive, deterrent; JCPOA Iran complied. Solutions from polls: nuclear energy not weapons, NWFZ, end threats (majority US and Iran); Cirincione; experts vs public; Arab states for NWFZ, Iran, G77; US blocks (Israel nukes, US law); Resolution 687 para. 14 obligates US/UK to ME WMD-free zone.
