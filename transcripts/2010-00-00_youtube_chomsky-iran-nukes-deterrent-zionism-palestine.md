## tl;dr

Q&A: audience asks what to do when a country is openly developing nukes and threatening to wipe out another country. Chomsky: What about countries that already have nuclear weapons outside the NPT and daily violate Security Council and IAEA? "Nor is Iran" openly threatening to wipe out Israel. The "Israel should no longer exist" line goes back to Khamenei in the US–Iran–Israel alliance era; Israel didn't care then. It means Israel should not exist *as now*; he agrees (single democratic state, like many in Israel); that's not calling for wiping anyone out. Iran has supported international consensus on two-state settlement. The two that are not only saying a nation shouldn't exist but destroying it are the US and Israel re Palestine, day by day (Gaza, West Bank), with our support. Official Israeli position ~20 years ago (Labor-led coalition): there cannot be an "additional" Palestinian state between Jordan and Israel (i.e. Jordan is the Palestinian state). Analogy: if Ahmadinejad said Jews don't need Israel because they have New York, we'd call it Nazism; that was official US–Israeli position. Nobody wants Iran to have nukes; regime is horrible but worse in region are our allies (vs Saudi, Iran looks like civil rights paradise). Solution: nuclear weapons free zone. Israeli strategist Martin van Creveld after Iraq invasion: if Iran is not developing nukes they're crazy (US said we'll attack anyone; you need a deterrent). Maybe they're developing a deterrent. No serious analyst thinks Iran would use nukes (country would be vaporized; clerics not suicidal); same for leaking to others. Israeli literature: real threat of Iranian nuke is not to Israel but to Zionism (if region too dangerous, educated/wealthy will leave). Thank you.
