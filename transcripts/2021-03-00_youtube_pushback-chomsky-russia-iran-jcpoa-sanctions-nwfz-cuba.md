## tl;dr

Pushback (Aaron Maté) with Noam Chomsky; book *Chomsky for Activists*. US–Russia: Biden renewed New START (hours before expiry); then bellicose rhetoric, sanctions, intel report on 2020 interference; Biden “Putin has no soul/killer,” Russia recalled ambassador. Chomsky: Russia threat core since 1940s; charges “don’t amount to much”; Trump’s “are we so innocent / lot of killers” was right; US interferes up to overthrowing governments (Palestine 2006, Yeltsin 1996). Iran: WSJ Israel attacking vessels with Iranian oil to Syria; Biden pledged return to JCPOA but adopted Trump position fully; Iran abided, US never did; Trump dismantled it, third-party sanctions; Biden demands Iran accept US violation and new harsher treaty or sanctions continue; piracy blocking Iranian ships. Foreign Affairs: maximum pressure “hardened Iran,” middle class to poverty; sanctions goal = devastate economy, “success.” History: 1953 coup, new docs; 1979 Carter/Heuser encouraged military to put down uprising; Nimrodi, Brzezinski; Saddam, chemical weapons, Halabja/Reagan blamed Iran; Vincennes 290, medals; Bush Iraqi nuclear engineers, Dole to Saddam April 1990; JCPOA under Obama; Pompeo at UN “you are renewing sanctions”; only US sanctions have bite. Cuba: unanimous UN opposition, pandemic internationalism, blocking Panama/Cuban doctors, Brazil/Russian vaccines; State Dept “successful defiance”; Iran and Cuba = state interest to crush defiance overrides big capital (agribusiness, pharma, energy). NWFZ Middle East: easy solution, one barrier US blocks to protect Israeli nukes (Symington etc.); can’t enter mainstream. JNF tax-free, settlements subsidized by US taxpayer. Dohan (UN rapporteur) on Syria/Venezuela sanctions illegal, no US media. 1.9T relief: accomplishment given McConnell/obstruction, child poverty measures good but temporary; Republican lockstep opposition.
