## tl;dr

Chomsky: not a sharp contradiction. US has never had serious concern about Iranian nukes: if Iran had a weapon it could not use it (country would be vaporized); deterrent is the threat, and deterrent is intolerable to US and Israel, who want to "rampage freely"; mafia principle (Godfather, successful defiance; Cuba, State Dept, Monroe). Under Shah US supported Iranian nuclear program (MIT, faculty approved, students opposed); Bush invited Iraqi nuclear engineers 1990; Israel has nukes. Iran independent + Hezbollah etc. = intolerable. Nides comments extreme (first time US openly "do what you like"); Israel conducts terrorism in Iran (killing engineers, sabotage); Israel cannot do major strikes without US penetration bombs and refueling; Netanyahu avoiding jail; Smotrich (civil administration of occupied territories first time, weakens "no occupation" pretense), Ben-Gvir. 84% enrichment: media from government; Burns said no weapon; real issue is deterrent. Khamenei fatwa on chemical weapons (Gareth Porter). Doctrinal orthodoxy: Nord Stream (NYT Ukrainian divers, thief-thief, US obvious); 20 years Iraq – cannot find one sentence saying US aggression (Nuremberg supreme crime); Vietnam same; "unprovoked invasion" only used for Ukraine (because it was provoked, projection); Global South; Bundy "wild men in the wings"; Samantha Power genre; Harvard Kenneth Roth, Iraq humanitarian intervention debate; censorship by omission. Israel strategy keep Iran threat high, US unconditional; Iranian government vs population; Iran told Saudi/UAE would destroy oil centers if directly attacked. Mahsa Amini protests; regime-change risk, 1953. NWFZ: Israel ambiguity (US law would bite), everyone in world in favor, US blocks (Obama), NYT Persian Gulf NWFZ (thief-thief); JCPOA inspections worked. US public opinion shifting (evangelicals, far right support Israel; McCollum, Sanders conditioning aid). Diego Garcia, African NWFZ. JCPOA revival unlikely; Iran in Shanghai Coop, Russia, Asia; Europe opposes sanctions but observes them; government vs business (1953 oil 40%, Cuba, "executive committee of ruling class"). Marshall Plan oil-based Europe (Kennan veto), Gorbachev common European home, Putin handed Europe to US, NATO Indo-Pacific, US vs China, Netherlands lithography; Ukraine only winner is US. Nuclear power and proliferation, waste, Somalia.
