## tl;dr

IRGC announced the 21st wave of Operation True Promise: combined drone and missile attack on Tel Aviv and central Israel. Attack package included the Khaibar Shakan missile (cluster munition warhead). Videos show cluster munition detonating over central Israel, submunitions across a wide area. Sirens across Tel Aviv and central Israel; seventh or eighth attack on Israel since midnight local time. No casualties reported yet (Israeli ambulance service). Khaibar Shakan means "fortress buster"; operational since 2022; maneuverable warhead, about 20 submunitions on descent, ~2.5 kg explosives each; spread over ~8 km squared. Iran has used this weapon several times in the conflict; Israel has been on receiving end since war started last weekend. Cluster munitions banned under international treaty but only for signatories; neither Israel nor Iran are party to the cluster munition convention.
