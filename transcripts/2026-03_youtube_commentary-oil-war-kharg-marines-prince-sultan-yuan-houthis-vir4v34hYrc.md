"90% of Iranian oil goes through Kharg. So any attempt to invade and destroy it would be not just a major escalation but a huge war crime." "Critical turning point."

**KC-135 / casualties:** Two KC-135 hit over Iraq 48 hours ago — one landed Ben Gurion (Israel), other crashed western Iraq, all crew died (11 at that time). No more because Iran will make sure that these bases are inoperable."
