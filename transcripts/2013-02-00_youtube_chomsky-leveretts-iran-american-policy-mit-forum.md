## tl;dr

MIT Technology and Culture Forum: "Iran and American Foreign Policy" with Hillary Mann Leverett, Flynt Leverett, and Noam Chomsky; moderator Ali Banuazizi. Intro: US–Iran history (missionaries, Baskerville, Shuster, 1946, 1953 coup, Shah, revolution). Hillary: On eve of 9/11 every ME government pro-US or anti-Iranian; today Egypt, Tunisia, Libya, Lebanon, Palestine, Turkey, Iraq pursue independent policies, more open to Iran. Cause = US imperial determination (Middle East oil for global primacy, not US consumption); 1953, Nasser (Arab oil, Non-Aligned), Camp David; 1967 Israel destroyed secular nationalism; post–Cold War unconstrained (1990, Fifth Fleet, Iraq sanctions, peace process). Iran tried to cooperate (1991, Lebanon, Bosnia, energy, Afghanistan/Al-Qaeda); US refused, axis of evil. Iran soft power; Hamas/Hezbollah/Iraqi Shia/Muslim Brotherhood win elections; nuclear = right to enrich, US demands surrender; only Islamic Republic can achieve this; need Nixon-to-China realignment. Flynt: Iran soft power strategy, 15 neighbors; pick political winners; nuclear = NPT right, US/UK/France/Israel no legal basis; Arab publics support Iran; balance of influence; 30 years of collapse predictions false; Khatami; come to terms. Chomsky: Read Going to Tehran; Washington Institute (Indyk, Ross, Abrams) "few months then war"; three questions: who believes threat (US/few allies, not non-aligned or Arab publics), what is threat (US intel: defensive, deterrent intolerable), solution (NWFZ; Helsinki Dec Iran would attend, Obama cancelled); 60 years "torturing" Iran; Abrahamian coup book; MIT–Shah nuclear; Vincennes, Bush, Iraqi engineers; John Snow Chatham House; Trias HIV/AIDS. Q&A: change (presidential leadership, public mobilization); Pakistan pipeline (secondary sanctions); Green movement (Leveretts: US uses disaffected elites, Turkey more journalists, focus undercuts diplomacy); Banuazizi (don't distort Iran, Mousavi/Karroubi, prisoners); fix (engage, NWFZ, Israel–Palestine); Afghanistan (Hillary: Iranian help, Northern Alliance, Bonn, Karine A, axis of evil); Syria (audience: Iran co-leading war; Chomsky: US drone terror).
