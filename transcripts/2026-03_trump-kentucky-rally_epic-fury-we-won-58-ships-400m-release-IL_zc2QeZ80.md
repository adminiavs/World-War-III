## tl;dr (Iran war segment)

**Victory narrative:** "We had to take a little trip to get rid of some evil, very evil people. It should have been done. FOR 47 YEARS, they've been killing our people." Operation Epic Fury — "Is that a great name? ... We've won. Let me tell you, we've won. ... In the first hour it was over, but we won." He chose the name from a list of ~20 options: "I said, I like that name."

**Navy / mines:** "We knocked out 54 ships in two days" (later "58 naval ships"). "We knocked out their navy." Knocked out 31 mine layers. Suggested using "the weapon that we use on the drugs that come in by sea" (drugs by sea down 98%); "they said, We'll give it a shot. It worked very well." Knocked out 28 or 29 mine layers — "they'll all be gone. By the time I'm finished speaking, they'll be gone." Asked why not capture Iranian ships for US Navy; told they wouldn't qualify; one general: "Sir, it's a lot more fun doing it this way."

**Oil:** "Earlier today, the International Energy Agency agreed to coordinate the release of a record 400 million barrels of oil from various national petroleum reserves around the world, which will substantially reduce the oil prices as we end this threat to America and this threat to the world." "Oil prices are already coming back down and it's going to come down. But we're not leaving until that job is finished. And it's going to be very fast."

**Degradation claims:** "Over the past 11 days, our military has virtually destroyed Iran. Tough country. Their air force is gone. Totally gone. That took the better part of about three hours. They no longer have radar. They don't have anti-aircraft equipment. They don't have anything. Their missiles are down 90%. Their drones are down 85%. We're blowing up the factories where they're made, left and right." "NOBODY HAS EVER SEEN ANYTHING LIKE IT." References Soleimani, al-Baghdadi, Operation Midnight Hammer — "we totally obliterated Iran's nuclear potential. We obliterated it. They don't have nuclear potential." "We don't want to go back every two years ... We got to finish the job."

**Obama/Biden:** Obama Iran deal — 757 with billions of cash to Iran; deal gave Iran right to have nuke "as of three years ago"; "if I didn't terminate that deal ... they would have had nuclear weapons and this world would right now be a different place." Biden "had no clue"; Afghanistan "most embarrassing day"; "we're not going to count on having competent presidents. We have to assume we're going to have incompetent ones."

**Later in speech:** "If we won that war, which we have basically done, if we won that war in one day, HE SHOULD HAVE DONE IT FASTER. It's unacceptable."

**Domestic (abbreviated):** No tax on overtime/tips/Social Security; Massie "disloyal," "nut job," "disaster as a human being," voted against tax cuts and border security; endorsement of Ed Galrine (Navy SEAL, farmer) vs Massie; Save America Act (voter ID, proof of citizenship, no mail-in except illness/disability/military/travel, "best of Trump" — no men in women's sports, no transgender surgery for children); Nick Sandmann, Jake Paul; Gavin Newsom "admitted mental problems"; midterms critical.
