Self‑defense unlimited but only against military bases/installations, not Americans at home; if aggression stops, Iran stops. Rejects "proxies": groups fighting just cause, independent, we support them; we don't need them for defense. Nuclear: 2015 deal proved goodwill, US withdrew; 2025 again we negotiated in good faith, then attack; religious verdict (supreme leader) and security doctrine against nukes; one who must prove goodwill is US. Protest question: Dec 28–Jan 7 peaceful protests tolerated; Jan 8–10 "terrorist operation," armed elements from outside (Mossad), shot at police and people to inflate deaths and draw US in; 3117 total, ~2500 martyrs, 200 police; plot linked to "12 Day War." Regime change "mission impossible"; 30 million at 47th revolution anniversary; 47 years of US attempts (coup, sanctions, Saddam war, snapback, terror) failed. Message to people: we are great nation, know how to defend, will survive stronger. Back channels: none now; de‑escalation if aggression stops first. Concessions: everything to prove peaceful program, not give up enrichment right; enrichment for needs (e.g. Tehran Research Reactor, 2 million Iranians); robust verification; Trump missed deal. Missiles: range kept under 2000 km by design, no US homeland capability, no intention; defensive/deterrence; confirm not building missiles to reach US. US bases attacked because they are attacking us. Strikes did not destroy missile capability; launching within an hour. Contact with Russia, China, Turkey, Pakistan, GCC; mission to end war.
