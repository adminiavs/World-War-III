## tl;dr

Aaron Stein (Foreign Policy Research Institute) discusses Wall Street Journal report that the US is running short of critical munitions, especially interceptor missiles, amid Ukraine drawdown and Middle East demand. Risk: more Iranian missiles get through; pressure on US/Israel to destroy launchers on the ground, aligning with four- to five-week timeline. Iran not achieving objectives either; UK use of Diego Garcia for defensive strikes noted. Quasi regime change already (Khamenei killed); goal to degrade military and coerce successor into a deal. Boots on the ground unlikely (unpopular, Congress). Gulf states may respond; Qatar shot down two Iranian aircraft. Endgame unclear when bombing stops.
