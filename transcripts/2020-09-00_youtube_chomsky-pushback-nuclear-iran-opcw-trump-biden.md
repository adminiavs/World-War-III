## tl;dr

Pushback (Aaron Maté) with Noam Chomsky. Trump nuclear policy: killing New START (last treaty), INF and Open Skies already gone; Billingslea "spend adversaries into oblivion"; ABM on Russian border; Trump principle: any agreement he didn't create must be destroyed (Paris, JCPOA, COVAX, biodiversity); INF scrapped then US immediately deployed new weapons; tactical nukes, hypersonic, space; Doomsday Clock to seconds; William Perry "doubly terrified" (threat greater than Cold War, nobody paying attention); Soleimani killing as if Iran killed Pompeo plus general at Mexico City airport; risk of incident in Gulf, Israeli strike, Iranian strike on Saudi oil, Israeli nuclear response. Democrats ignore Trump's nuclear hawkishness (Russia narrative). Reagan-era mass mobilization forced INF; now no equivalent. Trump not amenable to pressure (two constituencies: wealth/corporate, adoring base); Nixon/Gore conceded; vote against Trump = vote for Biden, then keep pressure (Obama 2008 marketing, activists went home; Biden less charismatic, Bernie movement). Pompeo at Security Council: demanded renewal of UN sanctions on Iran, Council refused; Pompeo said "it's reinstated because we say so"; no precedent, little comment; third-party sanctions. Iran options: strengthens hardliners; if Iranian threat were serious, simple fix = nuclear-weapons-free zone (Arab states, Iran, G77, Europe for; Israel and US block; US cannot acknowledge Israeli nukes or Symington bites; "all to protect US aid to Israel," unspeakable). "Malign behavior" = successful defiance (Cuba State Dept phrase, Monroe, mafia; Iran since 1979, US backed Saddam invasion, Reagan, Bush invited Iraqi nuclear engineers, sanctions). OPCW: Douma inspectors said evidence pointed to staged attack; findings censored; José Bustani blocked at Security Council from testifying for inspectors; US/allies don't want evidence discussed; Bustani ousted by Bush over Iraq.
