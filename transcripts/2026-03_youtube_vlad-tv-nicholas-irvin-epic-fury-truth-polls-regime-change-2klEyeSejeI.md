## tl;dr

**Timing / prediction:** Breaking interrupt for Iran war. Irvin on last appearance gave "3 to 6 months" for a strike; "day after the interview dropped … it popped off." "Way sooner than I predicted." He'd said "by the time this interview airs I wouldn't be surprised if there was a strike" (didn't assume US+Israel). Buildup in media "prep you … desensitize you"; "warning signs … they're prepping us because we're about to strike."

**Epic Fury / Midnight Hammer:** Operation Epic Fury = US name; Israel has "Roaring Lion." Earlier "Operation Midnight Hammer" (Iran nuclear facility, "allegedly"); Trump: "We completely obliterated Iran's nuclear potential." "Fast forward a few months … Iran was weeks away, days away … almost untouchable on nuclear program. I thought Midnight Hammer obliterated it. That was a setup, precursor to where we are today." Maybe "get reaction of American people … top secret mission Tom Cruz style." "Might have been a pile of dirt, hole in the side of a mountain." Bombs and mountains: Afghanistan 2001–02, Bin Laden in mountains — "you can bomb and bomb … embedded in mountain deep underground, only so much you can do." Suspects US let Bin Laden get away to prolong war (two Delta operators suspect same). "These people are built different … fighting since before I was born … planning since 1940s–50s." If you're smart you "move your stuff underground off radar." "Do I think we hit anything? I don't know." "Obliterated their nuclear program? I didn't think so from the get-go." "First thing that dies in war is the truth."

**Truth / media:** Chow hall: "another unit did x y z, this many killed … unit was wrong, casualties wrong … on Fox News, CNN … I was just on that mission." "Watched it multiple times." "If I were an American I'd be questionable about the numbers." Same now: "allegedly obliterated" then "we're at war … specifically because they have makings of WMD. What's right? What are we supposed to believe?"

**2003 Iraq / same playbook:** 2002–03 "same talking points" as now. Studied war since middle school. "What got us into that war was one big lie. American people haven't had enough time to digest … put feet to the fire … you sold us a lie, thousands killed, over a million Iraqis civilians." "Those same talking points … happening right now." North Korea has nukes "we don't touch them. We keep going after the same brown people in the desert." Netanyahu "40+ years … weeks away, days away … obliterated, set back to stone age … couple weeks later about to get it again. Same talking point." "Is it for our freedom or somebody else's?"

**Who benefits:** "I do know what's behind it. Starts with the letter I, ends with the letter L." (Israel.) "APAC … way too much pull, strings pulled on our politicians … they're nothing but actors. Paid actors. Producer in another country in the Middle East. They get paid to act like politicians, act like they have us in our best interest. They don't care about us the viewer. They care about the producer." "We don't have politicians. We have actors."

**Feb 28 / Khamenei:** "10 days ago" — 1:45 a.m. Eastern, US and Israel simultaneous massive joint air campaign, "over 2,000 targets that day." Khamenei killed. Supreme Leader "above president, above prime minister, above king. Literally second in command to God. Like the Pope." Thoughts: (1) What threat was he directly to US people? (2) Are we still in the game of assassinating foreign leaders? (3) "Call it not regime change but kill the regime leader and have say who the new supreme leader is — sounds like regime change to me." "Not calling it a war except Wednesdays and Thursdays. Other days it's targeted campaign. Equivalent of calling 911 — targeted attacks. Pearl Harbor was targeted attacks. When we do it, strategic attacks, targeted attacks. It's not war." (4) He was 86. How much more time did he have? Bin Laden "on dialysis in mountains … raid, no dialysis machine. I don't know. I don't believe anything. This was all about regime change but not in our benefit. Certain country wants to control entire Middle East. Wars we've been fighting since I can remember — all about the Middle East. Whose interest? 2003 Saddam, WMD — what threat? We don't know what we saw on Midnight Hammer. Satellite imagery, holes in the ground. Same satellite images 2003 — Powell, missiles, mustard gas, WMD — turned out to be nothing, made up. Same entity allowed to lie then and before. War mongerers — you never fought, you're a coward, recruitment station in every hood. I've tasted my friend's blood, fingers in bullet holes. Those people don't have to live with that. Trust: once they show you who they are, believe it. They don't change. Our government never went to rehab."

**Polls (81% Iranians don't like regime):** "I've never been polled. I don't know anybody who's been polled. These polls come from inside news organizations who spew propaganda. Who are they polling? Fox, CNN, Newsmax went to Iran to poll people who speak a different language? Did they poll Iraqis and Afghans? Polls aren't real. Same person who said believe the polls told you not to believe the polls last election when somebody else was favored. Hillary viewed on winning, polls astronomical, no chance Trump — those same polls that f***ed up royally are telling you now 81%. Polls are nothing more than [deception] to make you believe something that ain't true. One person out of a thousand got polled? Television tell your vision what to believe so when they do something you don't feel like the outcast."

**Terrorism / "good guy":** "State sponsors of terrorism — mix of both. Biggest contributor to terrorism … we got to stop looking at ourselves as the good guy. 250+ years we've only not seen war 14–16 years. Over 200 years continuous war. Killing innocent children, women, men. We've killed more innocent civilians than all those terrorist organizations combined. We call it cost of war — that makes it ok. Definition skewed — they say Allah Akbar, look that way so those are the bad guys. Magician's trick — look over here, behind my back I'm setting up to fool you again." "Bad guys — that's what they sell us. Sent us to do bad things to bad people. Job done, Pontius Pilate, wash hands. If they get you to believe they're bad guys they can tell you to do whatever, you have no conscience. I'm keeping America safe. They look at us the same way. We're the bad guys." News in Israel/Iran "completely different from news we get." Got out of bubble, traveled — "this is so ass backwards." Brainwashing; "can't get mad at me, I was under same spell." Breaking bubble "uncomfortable … shatter that would be detrimental to our sanity." CNN vs Fox — "all about what you believe … news is large broadcast … depending what side of compass you lie on … what you believe may be wrong, made up anyway."

**Iran strikes on Gulf:** 500+ missiles/drones at Israel and Gulf (UAE, Saudi, Qatar, Bahrain). "To hurt us. Attacking things we had over there — military bases, equipment, infrastructure. Not going after neighbors. Where the oil's at. F*** up that barrel, gas prices, shut down routes of transportation of goods and services." "It's not about killing them. It's about hurting us."

**Who has Iran's back:** "Russia. China." But Russia "f***ed up with Zelensky," years in that war, "can't give too much props." Iran's allies "may not seem like a lot but depending how you look at warfare — its people, people in other countries who sympathize, whole Muslim culture. People with same ideology and beliefs around the world — their biggest allies." Host: you'd want real allies with muscle, munitions. Irvin: "That's very conventional. We fought in Middle East 20 years. Afghanistan didn't have rockets, missiles, drones. You don't need much. Vietnam — guerrilla warfare, few IEDs, VBIDs, shootings, massacres. Airports evacuated bomb threats. Doesn't take much to shut down a country. To win a war you don't need ICBMs, nukes. Few people who believe in your cause, wreak havoc, willing to die for it."

**New York IEDs / domestic:** "New York officials confirmed Sunday two devices recovered during protests over Iran war were actually IEDs. Thank God they didn't go off." Irvin: "Last year, year and a half ago I said this was going to come to our country. Sit back and wait for sleeper cells, terror cells to do their thing. 100% believe it's going to happen within the next two years. IEDs, shootings. 100%. We're just getting started. Intro. 18–19 year old kids believed in the cause. When that demographic … pick up arms, play by their rules — guerrilla warfare we're f***ed. Only so much you can do to prevent it — involves you to become a gorilla too. Average person doesn't want to get that dirty. They know they're going in to die, get arrested, get shot. Martyrdom. Whatever they believe is out there — 72 virgins or $72 million — has more power than this reality. We're too caught up on next iPhone. They don't think that way. We're not willing to die for what we believe. If we did our government wouldn't be where it's at. We're too divided. After division comes conquering. We've already been conquered. No more we the people. Voting won't do it — same people, same system. Only one way to cure a forest — trimming hedges don't work." Founding fathers were called terrorists. "We've already lost."

**Faith / evolution:** Host: why pray it never comes here? "Bible — great tribulation. Those who believe in higher power, Yeshua, Yahweh — stop being scared. It's already been written. No weapon formed against you shall prosper — man-made. Nothing to fear. Ego keeping us separate from Most High's will." Host: is this how you thought on the front line or evolution? "Mix of both. Before every mission I prayed (500+ missions, raids, shot at, RPG'd, mortared, IED'd). Back when I practiced Christianity — I still worship same Most High, I classify as Rastafari. Switch happened when pinned down by enemy sniper, nine-plus hour gunfight. Couple times I debated stand up and get head taken off to end it. Awake five days straight. First time I truly felt and heard the Most High. Put blame on Most High I shouldn't have. Wrestled like Jacob. Rebelled, 30s, wake-up call, spanked by God — only one true God. Evolution — living and experiencing to understand." Last guy in firefight "watched comrades drop, tossed weapon, dropped to knees — they believe Allah's will, if that's the will it's going to happen, they didn't have fear."

**Ego / demise:** Trump rejected ceasefire, demanding unconditional surrender. Hegseth: "We have taken out entire air force in Iran, B-52s flying over Iran." Irvin: "B-52s not quiet — saying that is basically they have no air force left. Will it be our ego that leads to our demise?" "Won't be our ego so much as our puppet master. That's going to be our demise. Hand, puppet master leading them all. Another country, entity controlling the strings, how we move. That'll be our demise. Historical figures warned — one written in the Bible, flipping tables — warned about entities that control, manipulate, pervert your way of thinking."

**Kristi Noem:** $294M ad campaign, no Trump, self-deport under oath, said had Trump blessing — Trump said no, fired. "Lying face" (lion face), "paid actress," "play dress up" as agents/troops. "Cutthroat game — endorse one minute, oust the next."

**Casualties:** Seven US servicemen (one died yesterday). Iran side "1,500 to 25,000" — "hellified spread." Irvin: "More than 1,500. Areas we're dropping — not small firecrackers, densely populated, high-rises, hotels. Wouldn't put it past edging closer to 5–10,000." "Whole reporting — lose entire family in blink of an eye, building hit, school incident. Numbers we're told never right — real number probably scary. Iraq over a million civilians. Can't fathom a million. Third world reporting, medical, internet shut down — no way 24–48 hours after to find out. We won't know for weeks, months. If 5–10k dead, imagine wounded — US six/seven dead, how many wounded? Double or triple. Last US troop wounded on the first, died a week later." (Like Benjamin Cop — saved Irvin's life, shot, seven days to die.) "What is it for?"

**Personal / one day:** Mosul 2007 mortared every single day three months; learned to sleep through it. Contracting first day 35–40 Ugandans lost to mortar. Last deployment IED — guy stepped on land mine, first question "check if my dick is still there." First day Mosul driving behind striker, 50–75 lb IED blew it, guy butt naked, diesel, "I hope I get it like that or don't feel anything or body intact so parents can see my face." Last firefight five days nine hours, debated put hand up to get medevac — "parents won't get open casket"; PL's chest opened "hamburger meat"; Cop hit, arterial spray 20 feet, tasted blood "pennies," "get these f*** kill them all"; volunteered to be first out of ravine, six bullets left, sprint first — "if I get hit I get hit, I'm f*** done." "One day. Few hours out of one day. Six deployments, over 500 missions." "Haven't had a good night's sleep since I got out. Not a night I don't wake up bad dream. Stuff behind the scenes people don't talk about."

**How it ends:** "How most of our wars intend on ending — kill as many as possible, take out leadership, take out son like we did dad, rest of family, almost nothing left. Create regime change. But looking back regime change never works. Iraq, Vietnam — never cut and dry. After first bullets fired everything's out the window. Hope no more US casualties, no more innocent lives, no more little girls bombed in schools. Hope can only get you so far when bombs are dropping. Might wrap in two more, three more months — but 'two weeks to stop the spread' turned into two years. Hope we're not presented another COVID — two weeks to stop the regime, drawn into endless pointless war."
