Iran/pro-Iran militia (spokesperson of "Kathmul Amba central headquarters" or similar) claimed responsibility: said American refueling aircraft was hit by air defense in western Iraq, forced to crash, "all its crew members were killed" (six crew). - **CENTCOM statement (quoted):** "US Central Command is aware of the loss of a US KC-135 refueling aircraft. The incident occurred in friendly airspace during Operation Epic Fury and rescue efforts are ongoing. Two aircraft were involved in the incident. One of the aircraft went down in western Iraq and the second landed safely. This was not due to hostile fire or friendly fire."

Third "simply isn't"; opposition fragmented; arming Kurds would lead to massacre by Republican Guard. "In for the long haul." Britain should be "deliberately cautious"; "deep reluctance" (Iraq 2003 shadow).
