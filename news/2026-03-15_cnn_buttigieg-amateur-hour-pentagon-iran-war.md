## Full story

Discussing the war with Iran, former Transportation Secretary Pete Buttigieg told Jake Tapper on CNN (15 March 2026): "I think President Trump thought he could get a better deal than the Obama administration did. He failed to get that better deal, and he went off and launched a war without planning." He said: "This is clearly amateur hour at the Pentagon and in the White House."
