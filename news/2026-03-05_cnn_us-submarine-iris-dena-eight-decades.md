## Full story

Sinking of Iranian warship off Sri Lanka is something a US Navy submarine has not done in over 80 years. Pentagon released periscope video; Araghchi identified IRIS Dena lost 2000 miles from Iran; Sri Lanka rescued 32, recovered 87; 130 sailors aboard. Hegseth: quiet death. Araghchi: atrocity. Dena was returning from India MILAN 2026. Carl Schuster: given Iran attacks on US-friendly countries frigate likely positioned to strike shipping; sinking justifiable as preventive. Alessio Patalano: Trump March 2 document on free flow through Hormuz can justify viewing frigate as threat; unclear if ship at war readiness or US gave warning. Parallels to Belgrano 1982. Last US sub sink of enemy combatant: USS Torsk, 14 August 1945. Araghchi: US will bitterly regret precedent; theatre extended to Indian Ocean. CNN, 5 Mar 2026. Brad Lendon.
