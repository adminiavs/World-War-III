## tl;dr

Foreign Minister Wong: Australia may offer military support to Gulf states under attack from Iran (e.g. defensive ISR, air defence) but will **not** participate in offensive action against Iran or deploy into a ground war. "This is not Iraq, and we are not the Howard government." Government considering requests from Gulf states; supports action to remove nuclear threat and prevent Iranian regional projection, but defers legality of strikes to US/Israel. PM Albanese and Wong both call for de-escalation while insisting Iran must not get nuclear weapons. Aligns with Canada (McGuinty: Canada will not participate): key US allies urging de-escalation and ruling out offensive/ground role.
