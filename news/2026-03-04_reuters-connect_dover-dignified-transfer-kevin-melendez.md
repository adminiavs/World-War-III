## tl;dr

Dignified transfer of the remains of U.S. Marine Corps Lance Corporal Kevin Melendez of Fort Worth, Texas, at Dover Air Force Base, Delaware, on March 4, 2026. Melendez died in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, in a non-hostile incident. The transfer was documented by Reuters photographer Ken Cedeno. Jeddah is in the theatre of Operation Epic Fury; the designation non-hostile may refer to accident or illness rather than combat.
