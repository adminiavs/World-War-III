# Iran attacks on Amazon data centers in UAE and Bahrain

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** AOL / Fortune
**URL:** https://www.aol.com/articles/iran-attacks-amazon-data-centers-170558573.html
**Tags:** aws, data-centers, uae, bahrain

## tl;dr

Iran struck three AWS data centers (two UAE, one Bahrain). Outages hit banking, payments, enterprise software. US military uses AWS; Fars said Bahrain site targeted for military-intelligence role. Experts say first deliberate airstrikes on data centers in a conflict; dual-use cloud means more such targeting likely.
