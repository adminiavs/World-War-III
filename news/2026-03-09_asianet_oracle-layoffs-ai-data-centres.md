# Oracle planning to cut thousands of jobs while expanding AI and cloud

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** Asianet Newsable
**URL:** https://newsable.asianetnews.com/amp/business/oracle-may-lay-off-thousands-as-it-ramps-up-ai-spending-articleshow-qgkpg0f
**Tags:** oracle, layoffs, ai, data-centres, ellison

## tl;dr

Oracle may cut 20,000–30,000 jobs, announcement as early as this month; one of its biggest restructurings. Goal: control costs from heavy AI and data centre spend; compete with AWS and Microsoft. Larry Ellison plan: new data centres globally for AI (e.g. OpenAI). ~$50B raise by 2026 (debt and equity) planned; cash flow pressure. Reviewing jobs across divisions; slowing or pausing cloud hiring. ~162,000 employees globally. Tech sector layoffs rising from AI data centre build-out (Microsoft cut ~15,000 last year).
