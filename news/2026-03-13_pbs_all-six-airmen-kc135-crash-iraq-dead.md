# U.S. military says all 6 airmen in refueling aircraft that crashed in Iraq are dead

**Date:** 13 March 2026
**Source:** PBS NewsHour / AP
**URL:** https://www.pbs.org/newshour/world/u-s-military-says-all-6-airmen-in-refueling-aircraft-that-crashed-in-iraq-are-dead

## tl;dr

All six crew aboard the US KC-135 refueling aircraft that crashed in Iraq are dead. The crash followed an unspecified incident involving two aircraft in "friendly airspace"; the other plane landed safely. The loss was not due to hostile or friendly fire. Circumstances under investigation. Brings US death toll in the war to 13 (7 combat, 6 in this crash).
