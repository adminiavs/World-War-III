# UN: more than 300,000 displaced in Iran and Lebanon — Israel evacuation order 900,000

**Date:** 2026-03-06
**Source:** The National / Reuters / UN (consolidated)
**URL:** https://www.thenationalnews.com/news/mena/2026/03/06/un-says-more-than-300000-displaced-in-iran-and-lebanon/
**Tags:** un, displacement, lebanon, iran, litani, humanitarian

## tl;dr

UN refugee agency: 300,000+ displaced across Iran and Lebanon; numbers expected to rise. Iran: 100,000+ displaced in first two days (communications blackout prevented exact count). Lebanon: ~400,000 from southern areas; Israel issued widest-ever evacuation orders — nearly 900,000 people (south of Litani, Beirut southern suburbs, Bekaa); 80+ villages. At least 30,000 in collective shelters; many slept in cars or traffic; some 22+ hours to reach safety. Boston Globe: Israel sent troops into southern Lebanon; Hezbollah "ready for open war."
