# The Iran war has put Asia on the brink of an energy panic

**Date:** 2026-03-08  
**Source:** The Economist  
**URL:** https://www.economist.com/finance-and-economics/2026/03/08/the-iran-war-has-put-asia-on-the-brink-of-an-energy-panic  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** Asia, Gulf, US, Europe  
**Tags:** oil, energy, asia, brent, petrol, lng

## tl;dr

Second week of US-Israeli war with Iran has "all but stopped" seaborne oil exports from major Gulf producers. Brent jumped 9% on 6 March to $93/bbl, up 30% over the past week. US motorists paying >$3.40/gallon (≈50¢ more than 27 Feb). European natural gas 64% higher than pre-war. Asia particularly exposed; stranded Gulf supplies "choking off the region's economies."
