## tl;dr

BlackRock shares fell more than 7% after the firm limited withdrawals from its private credit fund. The fund received over $1.2B in redemption requests; BlackRock is capping payouts. Antonio Pequeno IV.

---

## Full story

BlackRock said it would cap redemptions from its private credit fund after receiving more than $1.2 billion in withdrawal requests. The stock dropped over 7% on the news amid a broader selloff (92k jobs lost, oil surge, Iran war). Forbes 6 Mar 2026, Antonio Pequeno IV.
