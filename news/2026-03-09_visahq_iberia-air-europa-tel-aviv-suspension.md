# Iberia and Air Europa extend Tel Aviv suspension; only three Spain-Israel flights on 9 March

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** VisaHQ
**URL:** https://www.visahq.com/news/2026-03-09/es/iberia-and-air-europa-extend-tel-aviv-suspension-only-three-spain-israel-flights-operated-on-9-march/
**Tags:** spain, tel-aviv, flights, iberia, air-europa, easa

## tl;dr

Iberia Express cancelled Madrid–Tel Aviv until at least 28 March; Air Europa through 21 March. EASA guidance cites missile-defence activity over northern Israel and southern Lebanon. Of 34 Spain–Middle East flights scheduled 9 March, only three operated (Tel Aviv and Dubai). Refunds or rerouting via Paris, Athens or Istanbul. VisaHQ notes visa requirements for Cyprus, Greece, Türkiye for rerouting. Corporate travel: war-risk exclusions in travel policies; some require approval for Israel, Lebanon, Jordan.
