## tl;dr

Targets widened to civilian infrastructure: Bahrain accused Iran of striking a desalination plant (Iran had said US struck Qeshm desalination, "US set this precedent"). Saudi Arabia reported first deaths — projectile killed two (Indian, Bangladeshi), wounded 12 in Al-Kharj. Pezeshkian toughened tone, backtracked on apology to Gulf. Lebanon: over half a million displaced. Israel struck oil depots in Tehran; Red Crescent warned of toxic air and acid rain. CENTCOM: US does not target civilians. War death tolls: Iran ~1,230+, Lebanon 397+, Israel 11, US 6 (pre–seventh).
