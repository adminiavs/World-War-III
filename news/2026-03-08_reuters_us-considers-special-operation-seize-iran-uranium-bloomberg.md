## tl;dr

US President Donald Trump is weighing the option of deploying special forces on the ground to seize Iran's near-bomb-grade uranium, Bloomberg News reported on Sunday, citing three diplomatic officials briefed on the matter. Reuters could not immediately verify the report. See also Axios and SBS coverage of the same option (special forces to secure highly enriched uranium at a later stage of the war).

## Full story

March 8 (Reuters) - U.S. President Donald Trump is weighing the option of deploying special forces on the ground to seize Iran's near-bomb-grade uranium, Bloomberg News reported on Sunday, citing three diplomatic officials briefed on the matter.

Reuters could not immediately verify the report.
