# Trump Blasted for Golfing as More Die in His War

**Date:** 2026-03-08

**Author:** Vic Verbalaitis

**Tags:** trump, golf, dignified-transfer, dover, casualties, jeffries, newsom, domestic-reaction

## tl;dr

Trump was spotted playing golf at Trump National Doral in Miami on Sunday morning as Middle East updates continued. Video showed him wearing the same USA baseball cap he wore at Saturday's dignified transfer for the six service members killed in the war. House Minority Leader Hakeem Jeffries: "America is at war, TSA agents aren't being paid and this guy is playing golf." California Governor Gavin Newsom: "He's golfing after bombing children and raising your gas prices"; "A 7th American soldier is now dead. But don't worry, Donald Trump is golfing." CENTCOM announced a seventh U.S. service member had died from injuries sustained in Saudi Arabia on March 1. Six reservists were killed in the March 1 Kuwait drone strike; a later update noted a National Guard soldier died March 6 in Kuwait (health-related, under review). Trump had said March 1 "there will likely be more" Americans who die; "That's the way it is." Veterans of Foreign Wars advises removing hat at military funerals; Trump wore cap at dignified transfer, did not bow head.
