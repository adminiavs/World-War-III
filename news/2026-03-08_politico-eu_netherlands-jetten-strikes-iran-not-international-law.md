# Dutch prime minister says strikes on Iran not in line with international law

**Date:** 2026-03-07  
**Source:** POLITICO Europe  
**URL:** https://www.politico.eu/article/netherlands-rob-jetten-us-israel-war-iran-international-law/  
**Author:** Sonja Rijnen  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** Netherlands, Europe  
**Tags:** netherlands, international-law, jetten, europe-us, trump-surrender

## tl;dr

Dutch Prime Minister Rob Jetten said the U.S. and Israeli strikes on Iran were not in line with international law, while acknowledging the threat Tehran poses. "Both the attacks on Iran last week and the unacceptable retaliation from Iran on countries in the region fall outside the framework of international law." He has "understanding" for the initial strikes given Tehran's threat to regional stability but stressed the international community must defend international law and pursue diplomatic solutions. "The main question remains: when will the attacks be considered successful?" France has asked the Netherlands to provide support to the aircraft carrier Charles de Gaulle in the Mediterranean for defensive operations; Dutch government will consider over the weekend. Trump on Friday said his administration's ultimate goal is "unconditional surrender" by Iran's leaders.
