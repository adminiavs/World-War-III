# Shots fired at US Consulate in Toronto — treated as national security incident

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Source:** CP24 (Toronto)  
**URL:** https://www.cp24.com/local/toronto/2026/03/10/no-injuries-after-shots-fired-at-us-consulate-in-toronto/  
**Author:** Codi Wilson  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** Canada  
**Tags:** toronto, consulate, shooting, national-security, rcmp, csis, fbi

## tl;dr

Around 4:30–5:30am, two people in white Honda CRV stopped at US Consulate (360 University Ave), emerged, fired handgun at building, drove south. No injuries; heavily fortified building; bulletproof windows not penetrated. RCMP treating as "national security incident"; integrated gun/gang task force leading; RCMP, CSIS, FBI involved. Security heightened at US and Israeli consulates in Toronto and Ottawa. Comes after three synagogue shootings in GTA over weekend. Chris Lewis (analyst): "undoubtedly related" to Middle East conflict. Carney, Chow, Ford condemned.
