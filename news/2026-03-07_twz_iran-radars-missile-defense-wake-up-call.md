# Iranian attacks on prized missile defense radars are a wake-up call

**Date:** 2026-03-07  
**Source:** The War Zone  
**Authors:** Joseph Trevithick, Tyler Rogoway  
**URL:** https://www.twz.com/news-features/iranian-attacks-on-critical-missile-defense-radars-are-a-wake-up-call  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** US, Gulf, Iran  
**Tags:** thaad, an-tpy-2, an-fps-132, radar, jordan, qatar, uae, drone

## tl;dr

Iran repeatedly targeted high-value missile defence radars; attacks succeeded multiple times. Low-end kamikaze drones are main threat to radars that track hypersonic targets. AN/TPY-2 destroyed or badly damaged at Muwaffaq Salti Jordan; AN/FPS-132 Qatar damaged (one of three arrays, possible fire). UAE Al Ruwais and Al Sader, Saudi near Prince Sultan also targeted. Only 16 AN/TPY-2 produced (~250-300M each); Qatar radar package ~1.1B; years to replace. TWZ long warned drone threat to strategic radars. Small drone with grenade-sized charge can mission-kill array. Need layered defence of radars; argument for moving missile tracking to space. Trump Golden Dome needs space-based tracking; accelerating costly.
