# After AWS data centres in Dubai hit, Amazon and Microsoft may reroute West Asia workload to India

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** Times of India
**URL:** https://timesofindia.indiatimes.com/technology/tech-news/after-aws-data-centres-in-dubai-hit-why-amazon-and-microsoft-may-be-looking-to-reroute-west-asia-data-centre-workload-to-these-cities-in-india/articleshow/129328139.cms
**Tags:** aws, microsoft, india, data-centres

## tl;dr

AWS and Microsoft exploring redirecting West Asia workloads to India and Singapore. Capacity sought in Mumbai, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kochi for banking. March 2 drone strikes hit two AWS UAE and one Bahrain; 25 services disrupted, 34 degraded. India surplus capacity; 270B committed; 1.4 GW to 10 GW in 5-7 years. AWS urges customers to migrate Middle East workloads to alternate regions.
