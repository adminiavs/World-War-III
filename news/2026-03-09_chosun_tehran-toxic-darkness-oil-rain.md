# Airstrikes Plunge Tehran into Toxic Darkness, Oil Rain

**Date:** 2026-03-09  
**Source:** Chosun Ilbo (English)  
**Author:** Choi Hye-seung  
**URL:** https://www.chosun.com/english/world-en/2026/03/09/O6C7WHVAMJAN3DGJ2TE6W3NHO4/  
**Tags:** tehran, oil-depots, acid-rain, irgc, gulf-retaliation, mojtaba

## tl;dr

US-Israeli airstrikes on Tehran oil storage facilities (night 7th to morning 8th) caused toxic smoke, black oil rain, and doomsday darkness; residents told to stay indoors; fuel limit cut to 20L. Iran condemned attacks as war crimes; IRGC warned similar measures against Gulf oil facilities and said if the world can afford oil above $200, continue this game. Iran named Mojtaba Khamenei supreme leader.
