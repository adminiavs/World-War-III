# Prewar US intel assessment found intervention in Iran was not likely to change leadership

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** AP
**URL:** https://apnews.com/article/iran-intelligence-assessment-trump-ad20c1f1168d4318af516d7b19d372e7
**Tags:** nic, intelligence, regime-change, mojtaba

## tl;dr

National Intelligence Council assessment in February: neither limited airstrikes nor larger prolonged campaign likely to result in new government in Iran even if leadership killed. No powerful unified opposition poised to take over; establishment would preserve continuity if Khamenei killed. Undercuts admin claim of quick completion. Iran chose Mojtaba (hardline) Sunday. WaPo and NYT reported earlier. ODNI declined comment. Trump long skeptical of intel community. Goldberg: intel assessment almost like op-ed; cited Afghanistan and Ukraine intel misses.
