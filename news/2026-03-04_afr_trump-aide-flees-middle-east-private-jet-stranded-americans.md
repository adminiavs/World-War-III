## Full story

Washington | When you are fleeing a war that your former boss started in a foreign land, it helps to have friends in high places.

Trump adviser Alex Bruesewitz was in Doha, Qatar, when US-Israeli strikes on Iran began, and experienced a chaotic 72 hours scrambling to find a way out of the Middle East. Officials in the US, Qatar and Saudi Arabia helped Alex Bruesewitz escape the conflict, but hundreds of thousands of citizens are still there, and getting angry.

Jessica Gardner, United States correspondent. Updated Mar 4, 2026.
