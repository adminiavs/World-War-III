# Pentagon to move more Marines, extra warships to Middle East amid Iran tensions

**Date:** 13 March 2026
**Source:** KATV / The National News Desk (AP)
**URL:** https://katv.com/news/nation-world/pentagon-to-move-more-marines-extra-warships-to-middle-east-amid-iran-tensions-united-states-military-department-of-war-pete-hegseth-conflict-deploy-operation-epic-fury-israel-strait-of-hormuz-oil-gas-president-donald-trump

---

## tl;dr

Pentagon confirmation: Hegseth approved CENTCOM request for an "amphibious ready group" and its Marine expeditionary unit -- multiple warships and 5,000 Marines -- to join forces already in the region. USS Tripoli (Japan-based) redirected. Deployment comes as Iranian attacks on the Strait of Hormuz have intensified. US death toll at least 13 (seven killed in combat, six in the KC-135 crash). About 140 service members injured, eight severely. Named casualty: US Army Sgt. Benjamin N. Pennington -- killed in Iranian attack on Prince Sultan Air Base, Saudi Arabia, March 1. Dignified transfer at Dover Air Force Base on March 9.

---

## Key details

- **Approved by:** Secretary of War Pete Hegseth (CENTCOM request)
- **What:** "Amphibious ready group" -- multiple warships + 5,000 Marines total (USS Tripoli redirected from Japan)
- **Why:** Iranian attacks on Strait of Hormuz intensifying; commercial traffic disrupted; energy markets strained
- **US death toll:** 13 -- 7 killed in combat, 6 in KC-135 crash (western Iraq, "friendly airspace" collision)
- **Injuries:** 140+ wounded, 8 severely
- **Named dead:** Sgt. Benjamin N. Pennington -- killed at Prince Sultan Air Base, Saudi Arabia, March 1. Dignified transfer at Dover AFB, March 9.
- **Trump (Truth Social):** "We are totally destroying the terrorist regime of Iran... Iran's Navy is gone, their Air Force is no longer."
- **Hegseth:** Iran's leadership is "desperate and hiding." New supreme leader "wounded and likely disfigured."
