# US Treasury's Bessent to meet Chinese vice premier in France ahead of Trump-Xi summit

**Date:** 2026-03-12  
**Source:** Reuters  
**URL:** https://www.reuters.com/world/china/us-treasurys-bessent-meet-chinese-vice-premier-france-2026-03-12/  
**Region:** US, China, France  
**Tags:** bessent, xi-trump-summit, trade, paris  

---

## tl;dr

US Treasury Secretary Scott Bessent will meet Chinese Vice Premier He Lifeng in Paris 15–16 March; USTR Jamieson Greer also to participate. Aim: continue US-China trade and economic dialogue ahead of Trump-Xi summit in Beijing end of March. Planning for the leaders summit has been delayed and complicated by the Iran war; unclear whether there can be tangible outcomes beyond pledging to continue trade truce. New Section 301 investigations into excess capacity (announced Wednesday) could lead to new tariffs; Greer has spoken of need for more balanced trading relationship, reducing US trade deficit. Paris meeting under pressure to identify deliverables for the summit.
