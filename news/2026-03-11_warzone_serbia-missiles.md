# Serbian MiG-29 With Chinese Missiles

## Source
- The War Zone (10 Mar 2026)

## Summary

Serbia operating Chinese CM-400 supersonic missiles on MiG-29 fighters. Previously only Pakistan had this capability.

CM-400 is Mach 4.5 terminal speed, 155-250 mile range, 2000 lb weapon. Uses tablet fire control system for integration.

Serbia moving from Russia to China and Israel for arms. Unique regional capability.
