## tl;dr

SNL Weekend Update (Mar 8): Michael Che mocked Trump after the president was spotted with a large rash on his neck at a Medal of Honor ceremony. Che joked: Apparently someone accidentally washed his shirt in holy water. The line got long laughter and applause. White House told HuffPost the rash was due to preventative skin treatment, a very common cream on his neck for one week; redness expected to last a few weeks. Colin Jost questioned why Trump would fire Homeland Security head Kristi Noem; listed controversies including Noem describing Minneapolis residents shot by federal immigration agents (Renee Good, Alex Pretti) as domestic terrorists, and the $220M taxpayer ad campaign where she dressed as Jessie from Toy Story on horseback at Mount Rushmore. Jost made a dig about her alleged affair with an aide. Jost said on some level he feels bad for Noem: Imagine being singled out as the worst member of Trumps cabinet. Thats like someone saying youre making Diddy uncomfortable.
