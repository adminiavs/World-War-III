# Pentagon munitions cost estimate for Iran war

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Source:** The Hill  
**URL:** https://thehill.com/policy/defense/5776761-pentagon-war-munitions-estimate/  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** US  
**Tags:** pentagon, munitions, cost, epic-fury

## tl;dr

Pentagon cost estimates for Operation Epic Fury munitions. Washington Post: $5.6 billion on munitions in first two days alone (three officials to Congress); far exceeds initial $1B/day estimate. GOP lawmakers reportedly received estimates nearer $2B. Tomahawks ~$2.2M each; 2000+ munitions, 5000+ targets. Center for American Progress: $630M to assemble force before first shot; friendly fire (3 F-15s) ~$351M.
