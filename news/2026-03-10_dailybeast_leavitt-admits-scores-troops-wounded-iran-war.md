**Date:** 2026-03-10

## tl;dr

White House press secretary Karoline Leavitt admitted ~150 US troops wounded in Iran war (day 11). Pentagon later confirmed ~140 wounded; vast majority minor; 108 returned to duty; 8 severely injured. First public insight into broader toll. War objectives: eliminate nuclear capability, destroy navy, annihilate ballistic missile program. "Complete and unconditional surrender." Gas prices jumped 50+ cents since war began; Leavitt insists temporary. Trump shifting on Minab school strike; Leavitt defers to Pentagon investigation.
