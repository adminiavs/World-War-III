## tl;dr

US sent a military patrol aircraft through the Taiwan Strait (P-8A Poseidon, Wednesday 12 Mar), weeks before the Xi-Trump summit — first publicly reported transit this year. PLA jet manoeuvres reported near Taiwan after several days of pause. US Seventh Fleet: passage illustrated US commitment to "free and open Indo-Pacific." Trump planned visit to China 31 Mar–2 Apr to meet Xi; Taiwan likely on agenda. Beijing sees Taiwan as part of China to be reunited by force if necessary; US opposed to forcible takeover and committed to supplying Taiwan with weapons.
