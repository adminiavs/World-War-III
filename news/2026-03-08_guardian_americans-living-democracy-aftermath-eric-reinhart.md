# Americans arent facing a democratic collapse. Were living in its aftermath

**Date:** 2026-03-08  
**Source:** The Guardian (Opinion)  
**URL:** https://www.theguardian.com/global/commentisfree/2026/mar/08/trump-democracy-oligarchy-policy  
**Author:** Eric Reinhart (political anthropologist, psychiatrist, psychoanalyst)  
**Tags:** democracy, oligarchy, trump, us-politics, psychology

## tl;dr

Eric Reinhart argues the US was an oligarchy well before Trump; democratic collapse already occurred for tens of millions enduring precarious housing, inaccessible healthcare, debt servitude, exclusion from political power. Fixation on saving democracy is an illusion that satisfies a psychological need; elite narrative insists a functional democratic order is slipping away under a singular figure. Solution in this telling is preservation but to what democracy: settler colonialism, slavery, mass incarceration, corporate capture. Bipartisan deregulation and privatization transferred democratic power to corporations and billionaires. Genuine democracy requires public infrastructure: housing, childcare, schools, healthcare, unions. Grassroots organizing to protect immigrants from ICE is where democracy begins: people not abandoning one another. Chicago neighborhood example.
