# After initially voicing support, Trump says he doesn't want Kurds to enter Iran war

**Date:** 2026-03-07  
**Source:** The Times of Israel  
**URL:** https://www.timesofisrael.com/after-initially-voicing-support-trump-says-he-doesnt-want-kurds-to-enter-iran-war/  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** US, Iraq, Iran  
**Tags:** trump, kurds, kurdish, iraq, iran-war

## tl;dr

Trump reversed course: said he does not want Kurdish fighters from Iraq to join the war against Iran ("war is complicated enough"; "we don't want to see the Kurds get hurt or killed"). On Thursday he had said he would welcome Kurdish involvement ("wonderful that they want to do that, I'd be all for it"); Friday told Reuters it would be "wonderful" if they crossed the border. Kurdish officials had said Trump personally asked their leaders for assistance; reports of US-backed Iranian Kurdish op from Iraq. Trump also: blamed Iran for the girls' school strike (US probes said likely American); dismissed Russia-supplying-Iran intel; no settlement with Iran; wants to pick next leader; said "at some point I don't think there will be anybody left maybe to say We surrender"; war "will continue for a while longer"; ground troops "possibly... for a very good reason"; enriched uranium "could do later." Truth Social swipe at Starmer (carriers "we've already won").
