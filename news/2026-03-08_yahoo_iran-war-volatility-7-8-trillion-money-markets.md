## tl;dr

Iran war heightens volatility (oil, uncertainty, inflation risk). Bigger worry: 7.774 trillion in money market funds (Fed Q3 2025) all-time high. Since Sept 2024 Fed cut six times yet money market assets kept climbing; ultimate gauge of investor uncertainty; suggests fear of correction or crash. Shiller P/E 39-41, second-priciest in history; prior five times above 30 followed by 20-89 percent declines. Money market inflows preceded big corrections this century. Iran war: 20-21M barrels/day through Hormuz at risk; higher pump and heating costs; may reignite inflation and tie Fed hands. Conclusion: 7.8T figure objectively bigger worry; investors losing faith in stock market.
