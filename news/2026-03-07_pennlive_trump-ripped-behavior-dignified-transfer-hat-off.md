## tl;dr

Trump at Dover for dignified transfer of six US service members killed in Kuwait drew criticism for not bowing his head and for wearing a white USA baseball cap throughout the ceremony. Michael Steele (former RNC chair) on X: "This fool has ABSOLUTELY no sense of dignity or appreciation for the moment... Take your (expletive) hat off." Chris D. Jackson: "Trump just wore a campaign hat to a dignified transfer for fallen U.S. soldiers that were killed during his Iranian blunder"; contrasted with attacks on Biden for glancing at his watch. Others: "Disgraceful," "deplorable, callous"; one noted Obama not wearing a tie was a scandal while Trump's hat at Dover "it's crickets." Lists the six killed: O'Brien, Khork, Marzan, Amor, Tietjens, Coady.
