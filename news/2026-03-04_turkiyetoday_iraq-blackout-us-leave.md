## Full story

Iraq Ministry of Electricity announced a complete shutdown of the national power system on Wednesday. Every governorate lost electricity after all transmission lines and generating stations went offline. Cause remained unclear amid the escalating regional conflict. Iraq is caught in the crossfire of the US-Israeli campaign and Iranian retaliatory strikes. US Embassy Baghdad issued an urgent directive: US citizens strongly encouraged to depart as soon as safely able; leave Iraq now. State Department had already set Iraq at Level 4 Do Not Travel; non-emergency US staff ordered to leave March 2. Fresh explosions in Erbil; pro-Iranian groups including Saraya Awliya al-Dam claimed attacks near Erbil and American Victoria base Baghdad. Turkiye Today 4 Mar 2026.
