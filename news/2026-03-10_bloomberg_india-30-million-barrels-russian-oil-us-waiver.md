# India snaps up 30 million barrels of Russian oil after US waiver

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Source:** Bloomberg  
**URL:** https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-10/india-snaps-up-30-million-barrels-of-russian-oil-after-us-waiver  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** India / Russia  
**Tags:** india, russia, oil, waiver, hormuz, us-sanctions

## tl;dr

India buying 30 million barrels of Russian oil after US temporarily lifted some sanctions (Treasury license through April 4) to allow Indian purchases — war in Middle East sent oil prices surging. US placed sanctions on Rosneft and Lukoil in Oct over Ukraine. Damage control as Hormuz disruption constrains Middle East supply.
