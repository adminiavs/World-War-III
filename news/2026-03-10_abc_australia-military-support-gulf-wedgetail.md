# Australia to provide military support to Gulf states attacked by Iran

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** ABC News (Australia)
**URL:** https://www.abc.net.au/news/2026-03-10/australia-to-provide-military-support-to-gulf-states-/106435248
**Tags:** australia, gulf, wedgetail, uae, albanese

## tl;dr

PM Albanese confirmed ADF will deploy E-7A Wedgetail to Gulf for four weeks (long-range reconnaissance) and provide medium-range air-to-air missiles to UAE. 85 personnel deploy, operational by end of week. Rationale: ~115,000 Australians in Middle East, ~24,000 in UAE; helping UAE defend itself helps Australians. All six Gulf states impacted by Iranian strikes. Wong had confirmed requests for assistance from some countries on Sunday.
