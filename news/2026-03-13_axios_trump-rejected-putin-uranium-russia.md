# Trump rejected Putin offer to move Iran uranium to Russia

**Date:** 13 March 2026
**Source:** Axios
**URL:** https://www.axios.com/2026/03/13/iran-nuclear-uranium-trump-russia-putin

## tl;dr

Putin proposed moving Irans enriched uranium to Russia to end the war. Trump turned him down. Iran has ~450 kg 60% enriched. US: we need to see the uranium secured. Hegseth: US has a range of options; one is Iran voluntarily surrendering it. Trump: not currently a top priority but at some point we might be. Trump acknowledged Russia is aiding Iran.

**Impact:** Diplomatic path without ground troops was rejected; reinforces Rubio and Jiang on securing material.
