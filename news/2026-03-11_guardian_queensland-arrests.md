# Queensland: First Arrests Under Phrase Ban

## Source
- The Guardian (11 Mar 2026)

## Key Facts

Two protesters arrested on first day of Queensland hate-speech laws. Arrests at state parliament within hours of laws taking effect.

Under the act, up to two years jail for banned phrases including from the river to the sea and globalise the intifada if causing menace, harassment or offence.

Liam Parry (Students for Palestine) arrested moments after explaining the slogan. Second person arrested wearing shirt with the phrase.

Laws passed previous week. Constitutional challenge expected. Pattern of civil liberties erosion during wartime.
