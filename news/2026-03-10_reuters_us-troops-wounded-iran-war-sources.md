# Many as 150 US troops wounded so far in Iran war — sources

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Source:** Reuters  
**URL:** https://www.reuters.com/world/middle-east/many-150-us-troops-wounded-so-far-iran-war-sources-say-2026-03-10/  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** US  
**Tags:** casualties, wounded, pentagon, leavitt

## tl;dr

White House press secretary Karoline Leavitt acknowledged about 150 US troops have been wounded in the war with Iran. Pentagon later confirmed ~140 wounded; vast majority minor injuries; 108 already returned to duty; 8 severely injured. First official insight into broader toll; war in 11th day.
