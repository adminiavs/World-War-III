# IDF strikes IRGC aerospace HQ linked to satellites and missiles

**Date:** 2026-03-08  
**Source:** The Jerusalem Post  
**Author:** Yonah Jeremy Bob  
**URL:** https://www.jpost.com/defense-and-tech/article-889267  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** Israel, Iran  
**Tags:** idf, irgc, aerospace, satellite, khayyam, icbm

## tl;dr

IDF attacked Iran Aerospace Headquarters (IRGC) on 8 March; site used for satellite launches and potentially ICBM dual-use. Linked to 2022 Khayyam satellite (Russian Soyuz, Baikonur). Israel worried Khayyam and Russia-Iran space cooperation could boost ICBM and surveillance of Israeli/regional targets. Dec 2025 Tehran launched three domestic satellites from Russian site as Netanyahu met Trump. Post-12 Day War Iran nuclear program in shambles but satellite threat remains for ICBM progress, conventional ballistic threat, and surveillance. Israel has achieved surprise in current war; strike may remove or delay ICBM. Unclear why site not hit earlier.
