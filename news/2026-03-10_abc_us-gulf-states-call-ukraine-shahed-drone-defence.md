# US and Arab states turn to Ukraine for help against Iranian drone attacks

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Source:** ABC News (Australia)

**Tags:** ukraine, shahed, drones, interceptor, zelenskyy, gulf

## tl;dr

US and Arab states calling on Ukraine for help against Iranian Shahed drones. Zelenskyy received requests from US, Europe, 11 countries neighboring Iran. Ukraine world leader in counter-Shahed tech after four years vs Russia. Zelenskyy: sent experts and interceptor drones to Jordan for US bases; teams to Qatar, UAE, Saudi Arabia. Proposed swap: Patriot missiles for interceptors. Patriot ~$1M vs Shahed $20–50K; unsustainable. Ukraine manufacturers: General Cherry Bullet, Wild Hornets Sting, Skyfall P1-Sun (~$1K). Carnegie: "We do not have at scale good defences against drones." US reportedly unprepared for highly predictable threat.
