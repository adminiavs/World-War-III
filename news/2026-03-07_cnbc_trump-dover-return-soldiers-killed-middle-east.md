# Trump joins grieving families during return of soldiers killed in war in the Middle East

**Date:** 2026-03-07  
**Source:** CNBC  
**URL:** https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/07/trump-return-of-soldiers-killed-in-war-in-the-middle-east.html  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** US  
**Tags:** trump, dover, dignified-transfer, casualties, 103rd-sustainment

## tl;dr

Trump and Melania attended dignified transfer at Dover for six Army Reserve soldiers (103rd Sustainment Command) killed by drone strike in Kuwait on 1 March. Vance, Hegseth, Bondi, Wiles, Witkoff, Gabbard present; governors and senators from Iowa, Minnesota, Nebraska, Florida. Trump wore blue suit, red tie, white USA hat; did not speak; saluted as each flag-draped case was carried. Ritual lasted about half-hour. Quotes from families: Joey Amor (Nicole scheduled to return within days); O'Brien's aunt; Marzan's sister; Coady's father; Khork family; Tietjens' cousin. Trump later: "It's a very sad day," "glad we paid our respects," "great people, great parents." Notes Trump's previous Dover visits (Syria ambush, Yemen SEAL, Afghanistan).
