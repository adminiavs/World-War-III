# Trump's Iran War Is an Affordability Disaster

**Date:** 2026-03-08  
**Source:** Common Dreams  
**Author:** Brian Garvey (Massachusetts Peace Action)  
**URL:** https://www.commondreams.org/opinion/trump-s-iran-war-is-an-affordability-disaster  
**Content type:** opinion  
**Region/country:** US  
**Tags:** affordability, gas-prices, dow, strait-of-hormuz, voters

## tl;dr

One week in: gas prices up >11%, Dow erased all 2026 gains. "War tax at the pump"; 20% of world oil through Hormuz now shut. Oil affects whole supply chain — shipping, groceries. UMD poll: only 21% supported US strikes before first bomb. Trump had called Iraq "big fat mistake," vowed "no new wars" and "peace on earth" — then Venezuela raid and Iran war. "If we have to pay at the pump, the politicians who started this war should pay at the ballot box."
