## Full story

Social media influencers in Dubai have been warned they could face prison if they post content about the Iran conflict. UAE authorities told creators that posts deemed harmful to public order, national unity or state reputation could lead to fines up to 77,000 dollars or jail. Some influencers removed footage; one deleted a video of burning debris after an intercepted Iranian missile. Detained in Dubai said UAE cybercrime laws are among the harshest; wording deliberately broad. UAE public prosecution warned against publishing rumours from unknown sources; reposting subjects persons to legal accountability. Missile debris struck Fairmont Palm Jumeirah Saturday evening and near Dubai airport the next morning; also Burj Al Arab and Port of Jebel Ali. The Standard 6 Mar 2026.
