# Oil prices and markets seesaw over uncertainty of war as Iran names new leader

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** AP News
**Authors:** Jon Gambrell, Jamey Keaten, Samy Magdy
**URL:** https://apnews.com/article/iran-israel-us-march-9-2026-b2aa51ef51d4b57103ffee0c95bc2ff7
**Tags:** oil, mojtaba, markets, trump, casualties, lebanon

## tl;dr

Oil surged then fell; US stocks closed higher on hope war may not last long. **Mojtaba Khamenei** (56, secretive, close to IRGC) third supreme leader; appointment signals Tehran digging in. Thousands in Tehran and elsewhere pledged allegiance; "Death to America," "Death to Israel." **Trump:** no oil shortage; prices will drop soon; Venezuela shipments could help. Trump-Putin call Monday (Kremlin); Putin voiced ideas on quick political-diplomatic settlement. **Rubio:** US well on way to eliminating Iran ballistic missile stockpile and ability to produce and launch. **Kharazi (CNN):** Iran prepared for long war; no room for diplomacy unless economic pressure prompts others to stop US-Israeli aggression. **Casualties:** 1,230+ Iran; 397 Lebanon; 11 Israel; four UAE; seven US service members; two Israeli soldiers in Lebanon. UNICEF: 83 children killed, 254 wounded Lebanon since 2 Mar. **Israel:** ~1,900 IRGC killed (spokesman Defrin); did not rule out targeting Mojtaba; strikes Tehran, Isfahan, south Iran; 35 high-rises Beirut (Hezbollah); 700+ Hezbollah sites Lebanon. Turkey: NATO intercepted ballistic missile (second time). **Saudi:** Shaybah oil field drone attack; Riyadh said Tehran would be "biggest loser" if it continues. **UAE:** 15 ballistic missiles, 18 drones Monday; two wounded Abu Dhabi shrapnel. **Bahrain:** residential area hit, 32 wounded including children; refinery fire. State Dept: nonessential staff and families leave Saudi; 36,000+ Americans returned since 28 Feb.
