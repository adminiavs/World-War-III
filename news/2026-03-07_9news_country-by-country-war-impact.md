## tl;dr

Broadening war has ricocheted across the region; nearly every Middle East country sustained damage (missiles, drones, shrapnel). **Iran:** 1,230+ dead (Foundation of Martyrs); 160+ Minab school; hospitals, schools, 174 cities hit; Khamenei and top military killed; airspace closed. **Israel/Palestine:** 11 civilians dead; Beit Shemesh nine dead; airspace closed. **Lebanon:** 102 dead, 638 wounded (Tue); 84,000 displaced; Israel warned evacuate south and Beirut southern suburbs; ground combat; airspace not fully closed but many cancellations. **Kuwait:** 10+ dead (2 civilians, 2 Kuwaiti troops, 6 US soldiers); embassy compound struck, embassy closed; airspace closed. **UAE:** 3 civilians dead (Nepal, Bangladesh, Pakistan), 68 injured; ballistic missile + 6 drones hit; 131 drones repelled Thu; Amazon data centres targeted; airspace closed. **Bahrain:** 1 civilian dead; refinery hit; 75 missiles intercepted, 124 drones; airspace closed. **Syria:** debris injuries; airspace closed. **Iraq:** militia deaths; tanker hit near Khor al-Zubair; Irbil drones; oil field production stopped (Hormuz); embassy storm attempt; airspace closed. **Jordan:** 5 injured shrapnel; airspace open, cancellations. **Saudi:** 9 drones intercepted; US embassy Riyadh drones; Ras Tanura refinery targeted; airspace partially closed. **Egypt:** Suez rerouting hit economy. **Qatar:** 14 missiles + 4 drones Thu; Al-Udeid hit; airspace closed. **Oman:** 1 mariner dead (drone boat), 4 injured; Salalah, Duqm targeted; airspace open, cancellations; Muscat evacuation hub. **Cyprus:** British base hangar damaged (Shahed from Beirut). **Turkey:** NATO intercepted Iran missile; debris Hatay; airspace open. **Sri Lanka:** Dena 87 bodies, 32 rescued; second Iranian ship arrived (engine failure, 200 evacuated). **Azerbaijan:** accused Iran of drone attack on Nakhchivan (4 injured); Iran denied; southern sector closed. Two Australian sailors were aboard the US submarine that sank IRIS Dena.
