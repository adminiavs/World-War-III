# War Costs and Economic Impact

## Sources
- Fortune (10 Mar 2026)
- BBC (11 Mar 2026)

## War Cost Analysis

CSIS: Campaign costing $891.4 million per day. First 100 hours consumed $3.7 billion.

Penn Wharton: Two-month war could cost up to $95 billion.

Pre-war fiscal state: Federal debt past $38 trillion. Debt increased $1 trillion in two months (Aug-Oct 2025). US spending $1 trillion annually on interest alone.

Economic scenarios: Transitory shock means oil at $100, inflation up 35 basis points. Persistent shock means oil to $130, GDP reduced 1.5 percent, impacts through 2027.

## Maersk CEO on Shipping

BBC interview with Vincent Clerc: Costs will be passed to customers and consumers. Extra $200 per container equals 15-20 percent increase. 132 ships stuck in Gulf. Seven seafarers killed in Hormuz.

## Pentagon Spending Context

Fox LA: Pentagon $93 billion September 2025 spending included $6.9 million on lobster tail, $2 million on king crab, $15 million on steak, $124,000 on ice cream machines.
