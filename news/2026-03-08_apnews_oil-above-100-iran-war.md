## tl;dr

Oil eclipsed 100 per barrel for first time in over three and a half years. Brent 107.97; WTI about 106.22. Hormuz threat has all but stopped tankers. Iraq Kuwait UAE cut production. US gas 3.45 a gallon; diesel 4.60. Energy Secretary Wright said gas back under 3 before too long. Qalibaf warned oil impact would spiral.
