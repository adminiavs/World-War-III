## Full story

War entered seventh day. Israel claims near-complete air superiority, 2,500 strikes, 80% of Iran air defence destroyed. Friday over 400 targets in western Iran (ballistic missile launchers, drone storage). IRGC 23rd wave; Israel defence systems intercepting. After Khamenei killing, succession in question; Trump said he must be involved in selecting Iran next leader, Mojtaba unacceptable. Ali Larijani (Supreme National Security Council): Iranian forces waiting for potential US ground invasion; threatened to kill and capture thousands of US troops. US says Iran ballistic attacks down 90%, drones 83% since day one. Kuwait: US suspended embassy operations; air defence engaged. Bahrain: Iranian missile hit state refinery; fire contained. UAE and Qatar reported intercepts. CENTCOM (Cooper): last night Iran fired seven attack drones at civilian residential neighbourhoods Bahrain; unacceptable and will not go unanswered. CSIS: first 100 hours Operation Epic Fury cost about 3.7bn dollars (about 891m per day); 3.5bn not budgeted. Al Jazeera 6 Mar 2026, Elizabeth Melimopoulos and news agencies.
