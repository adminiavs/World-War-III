## Key Points

### Trump has no plan
- **"He has no plan. He's not really capable of formulating a plan, holding a plan in his head, acknowledging a plan and following a plan."**
- "He's an ad-lib guy... he's taken that mode of thinking and being and now put that into a war mode, which is a novelty to say the least. It may be actually a novelty in history. No one before him may have made up a war on a minute by minute basis."
- **"Having no plan becomes the plan."**
- "No one knows what I'm going to do next. So everyone is afraid of me. So that gives me maximum leverage."

### Contradictory statements
- "We're going to stop the war. We're going to start the war. We're going to bomb them back into the Stone Age. We're going to negotiate. We're going to have an unconditional surrender. No, unconditional surrender."
- Hegseth saying "the war has just begun... the best is yet to come" while Trump says "we've almost destroyed them. The war is almost over."
- "Two completely different stories. One from the president, one from Hegseth."

### Wall Street Journal story on advisers
- WSJ led with story that advisers are urging Trump to end war fast
- Wolff: **"It's not true. His advisers are not saying that to him. They are saying that to the Wall Street Journal as a way of advising him to end the war."**
- These are people "in the fairly close White House circle who are concerned... he doesn't know what he's doing. Therefore, nobody knows where this is going to go."
- They know "he is perfectly capable of saying it's ended. We've achieved all our goals. Victory is ours."

### Pentagon surprised by Iranian response
- "It's clear that the Pentagon and Donald Trump have been surprised by the strength of the Iranian response."
- Wolff: "This is still a regional war... I don't see the prospect of anybody else joining this war except to defend themselves."

### Mojtaba Khamenei
- "Son of Khamenei... more ferocious than his father"
- "His wife and his son were killed by Donald Trump and Netanyahu in this war"
- "Spent his life very close to the Iran Revolutionary Guard"
- "In control of the repression where they shot thousands of protesters earlier in the year. The very protesters that Donald Trump said help was on the way."

### Regime change impossible
- "That doesn't mean there's going to be regime change, which you might remember was a rationale about ten minutes ago for this war."
- Trump keeps asking "when is the regime going to change? When are they going to rise up?"
- "None of that is going to happen immediately, if ever. And it's very difficult to explain that to him. It's very difficult to explain anything. He doesn't want anything explained to him."

### Hegseth on 60 Minutes
- "Hegseth doesn't know what's in Trump's head"
- "Pete Hegseth knows he doesn't know what's going on. He's trying to manage the vagaries of Trump as everybody else is."
- "He can't tell anybody because he doesn't know, because Donald Trump doesn't know."
- Hegseth said "President Trump has a knack of mitigating risk" — Wolff: "nonsense"

### School bombing (Minab)
- Trump "said he thought it could be the Iranians' own Tomahawk missiles that took the school out. But the Iranians don't have any Tomahawk missiles."
- "It was probably our bombs... that is a horrible thing. I'm not sure it makes any difference to Donald Trump."

### Tucker Carlson
- "Tucker is on the phone all of the time... really in touch with all of the people he should be in touch with"
- "One of the main sources for journalists everywhere"
- "Talked to Donald Trump three times, at least three times before the war, advising him against doing it"
- Speculation Tucker may have been source for WSJ story

### Republicans worried — Doral meeting
- Gas prices up — "that is going to defeat very clearly a whole set of individual Republicans in November"
- "The biggest problem for the Republicans at this point? Donald Trump."
- "Those guys... want to concentrate on the issue that they find most daunting, which is affordability and the economy... Trump is clearly not addressing that and doing everything to exacerbate that problem."

### Trump could declare victory anytime
- "A virtue, if you want to call it a virtue, is that he's perfectly capable of walking away from this. 'We've accomplished everything we set out to do. We're coming home. It's a victory.'"
- "There is no victory... there is no goal. There is no achievable goal. But it's Donald Trump. So he can say, yes, it's victory because I say it's victory."

### UK/Starmer
- "Starmer looks like a guy who has no idea what's going on. No purchase on events, merely a bystander."
- Refused to let Americans refuel on British airbases
- British airbase in Cyprus was hit

### Timeline
- "You mean the war? No, you can't put a timeline on it. You cannot predict anything because it's Donald Trump."
- "It's very possible that tomorrow it's over with. It's as equally possible that six weeks from now, we're still having the same conversation."
- "At some point where it tips over and we can't get out. That's an alarming prospect."
