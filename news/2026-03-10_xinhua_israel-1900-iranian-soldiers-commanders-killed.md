# Israeli military says about 1,900 Iranian soldiers, commanders killed in strikes

**Date:** 2026-03-09 / 2026-03-10
**Source:** Xinhua
**URL:** https://english.news.cn/20260310/f26d6d1bed8346dab0e4539dbff9801c/c.html
**Tags:** israel, irgc, casualties, mojtaba, lebanon

## tl;dr

IDF spokesman Effie Defrin: about 1,900 Iranian soldiers and commanders killed since conflict start; Israel does not rule out targeting new supreme leader Mojtaba Khamenei. Second Israeli civilian killed Monday by Iranian missile (total 12 civilians, two soldiers in Lebanon). Health Ministry: 2,238 evacuated to hospitals, 91 still in hospital. Strikes continued Monday in Tehran, Isfahan, southern Iran; six military airports struck; 16 Quds Force cargo aircraft destroyed; 35 Hezbollah high-rises Beirut; 700+ Hezbollah sites Lebanon. Defrin: attacks will continue as long as needed; anyone who poses a threat will be struck.
