## tl;dr

Roughly seven months ago (Aug 18 White House meeting) Ukrainian officials offered Trump battle-proven tech to destroy Iran's Shahed drones — interceptor drones, air defense systems, sensors. Zelenskyy compiled PowerPoint. US official to Axios: "If there's a tactical error... leading up to this [war], this was it." Trump reportedly interested, "asked his team to work on it, but they have done nothing." Shaheds fly low and slow, difficult for US air defenses; US "burning" through precision missiles. EU defense officials: US no longer capable of supplying allies with missiles amid Iran war; continent must develop own manufacturing.
