## Country-by-country summary

### United States
- Trump tells Fox negotiation with Iran "possible... depends on terms"
- Hegseth: Tuesday will bring "most intense" strikes; "will not relent" until Iran "totally and decisively defeated"

### Israel
- Netanyahu: "not done yet," attacks "breaking their bones"
- IDF begins new series of airstrikes on Tehran
- Struck IRGC drone headquarters Monday night

### Iran
- FM Araghchi: prepared to continue missile attacks "as long as needed"
- New supreme leader Mojtaba Khamenei shows continuity of anti-U.S./Israel policies
- Araghchi: negotiations with U.S. "not on our agenda anymore"
- IRGC Navy Commander Tangsiri: will use missiles and submarines to stop U.S. fleet through Hormuz

### Iraq
- Civil Aviation extends airspace closure 72 hours
- 6 PMF members killed, 3 wounded in airstrike in Dibis district
- Foreign Ministry condemns attacks on diplomatic missions
- Two airbases (Victoria, Balad) targeted by drone attacks Tuesday evening

### Bahrain
- Intercepted 105 missiles, 176 drones

### UAE
- Ruwais refinery closed after drone attack
- Defense Ministry: 262 ballistic missiles, 1,475 drones, 8 cruise missiles detected
- 6 killed (UAE, Pakistan, Nepal, Bangladesh nationals), 122 injured

### Qatar
- Targeted by 5 ballistic missiles, all intercepted

### Saudi Arabia
- Aramco CEO warns prolonged disruption will worsen consequences
- ~17% of global oil supply normally transits affected region
- ~80 million barrels disrupted
- Ras Tanura refinery shut as precaution

### Jordan
- FM Safadi spoke with Iraqi FM Hussein on regional coordination

### Lebanon
- Death toll: 570 killed, 1,444 wounded since March 2
- 759,300 displaced
- Intense clashes along southern border
- Hezbollah struck bases near Tel Aviv, Safed, Haifa

### Cyprus
- FM Kombos: Europe should play active diplomatic role in de-escalation

### Turkey
- NATO Patriot system deployed in Malatya
- Erdogan told Pezeshkian any airspace violation "cannot be excused"

### Azerbaijan
- Sent humanitarian aid to Iran as olive branch after drone incident
