# Israel: Iran Missile Attacks Increasing, War Goals in Doubt

## Source
- Al Jazeera (11 Mar 2026): https://youtu.be/7KNWqtk08_0

## Key Developments

Iranian missile attacks on Israel increased in past 24 hours after period of decrease. Six rounds of missiles fired from Iran overnight plus Hezbollah rockets and drones.

Millions of Israelis in shelters overnight. "Sleepless night" as attacks targeted central and northern Israel.

Cabinet ministers forced into bunkers during security meeting.

## Siren Time Decreasing

Time between siren and rocket arrival "very minimal" and decreasing. 

Correspondent: "It might be because there is a jeopardy in the radar systems in the region that is not allowing Israel to detect the launch of missiles ahead of time."

## Israeli 80 Percent Claim in Doubt

Israel has claimed they destroyed 80 percent of Iran missile launchers.

But 20 launches fired from Iran in past 24 hours "casts a doubt on the Israeli narrative of destroying missile launchers."

"There are so many launches that Israel might not have detected or are aware of."

## War Goals Shifting

Israeli media talking about "war goals not being achieved the way the government said."

Change of tone from Israeli defense analysts and military sources: "Most likely they're not going to achieve the goal of changing the regime but they might be looking at undermining its ability to send threats into Israel."

"They are trying to make their words similar to that of Trump's as he was signaling that the most priority is to undermine the regime and not necessarily change it."

Some Israeli media now saying "maybe the regime will fall after the war ends" - a retreat from immediate regime change goal.

## Israel Wants to Extend War

Israeli military officials "all want to extend the war for at least two weeks according to security officials."

Strategy: "Hit targets much more intensely now, as if the war is going to end soon, while trying to lobby for more time to reach war goals."

Israel receiving "conflicting messages from the US president."

## Defense Budget

Israel expanding defense budget by approximately $13 billion.

30 percent of total budget now allocated to defense - cuts from other sectors.

Signal that "Israelis want to show they are not done yet."

## Key Quote

Security official to Israeli newspaper: "They will hit targets much more intensely now, as if the war is going to end soon, while they're going to try and lobby for more time to reach the war goals."
