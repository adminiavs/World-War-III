## Full story

Iranian Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi told NBC Nightly News (Tom Llamas) from Tehran that Iran is ready for a US ground invasion: No we are waiting for them. Because we are confident that we can confront them and that would be a big disaster for them. He refused negotiations with the US and said Iran had not asked for a ceasefire; in the 12-day war last June it was Israel who asked for an unconditional ceasefire. On the Minab school attack Araghchi said 171 children were killed and the American and Israeli militaries were responsible. Last Thursday he was in Geneva negotiating with Trump envoy Steve Witkoff and Jared Kushner; the attack while negotiations were ongoing has soured Iran on future talks; no communication with Witkoff or Kushner since. We have no positive experience negotiating with this administration; they attacked us twice in the middle of negotiations. On outcome: There is no winner in this war. Our win is to be able to resist. Succession: Assembly of Experts will choose new supreme leader; process could take longer due to conflict. He dismissed the Axios report that Trump said he needs to be involved in selecting the next leader: That is absolutely the business of Iranian people and nobody can interfere. NBC News, 5 Mar 2026.
