# Leavitt Confronted on Trump Fabrications

## Source
- The Daily Beast (10 Mar 2026)

## Key Exchange

CBS Chief White House Correspondent Nancy Cordes asked about Trump claiming Iran would attack US within seven days then bumping it down further.

Cordes: There are no US leaders or Israeli leaders making those same claims. Is he making this up to justify his decision to go to war now?

Leavitt: The president is not making anything up, Nancy.

Trump (Monday in Florida): Within a week, they were going to attack us 100 percent.

Leavitt: This was a feeling the president had based on facts.

Trump also suggested Iran struck the school with Tomahawk, but when confronted retreated: Because I just do not know enough about it.

Pentagon has told Congress there was no intelligence suggesting Iran was planning to attack first.
