## Full article

The Pentagon has approved moving an additional 5,000 Marines and sailors into the region (Wall Street Journal). The request was made by US Central Command and approved by Secretary of Defence Pete Hegseth.

The troops of the 31st Marine Expeditionary Unit are attached to the USS Tripoli, an amphibious assault ship usually based in Japan. The 31st MEU specialises in amphibious invasions. In order for the US to have substantial numbers of troops on the ground from an amphibious invasion, they would need a vessel like this.

Already there are 50,000 US troops in the region. Trump has not confirmed whether a ground invasion will take place.

US death toll rose to 13 after all six crew of the KC-135 refuelling aircraft that crashed in western Iraq were confirmed dead. CENTCOM said the crash was not due to hostile or friendly fire.

Hegseth: "With each passing hour -- we know, and we know that they know -- that the military capabilities of their Evil Regime are crumbling. They can barely communicate, let alone coordinate. They are confused; we know it. Our response: we will keep pressing. Keep pushing. Keep advancing. No quarter. And no mercy for our enemies."

Trump on Truth Social: "We are totally destroying the terrorist regime of Iran, militarily economically, and otherwise... Iran's Navy is gone, their Air Force is no longer, missiles, drones and everything else are being decimated, and their leaders have been wiped from the face of the earth. We have unparalleled firepower, unlimited ammunition, and plenty of time."
