## tl;dr

ABC explainer cataloguing Trump administration's shifting and contradictory statements on Iran war. Key contradictions: (1) Regime change — Trump urged Iranians to "seize control" (Feb 28), Hegseth said "not a regime-change war" (Mar 2), Trump told Axios "I have to be involved in the appointment" of new leader (Mar 5), references Venezuela formula; (2) Who struck first — Waltz invoked Article 51 self-defense, Rubio said US knew Israel would strike first so launched preemptively, Trump contradicted Rubio saying "I might have forced Israel's hand"; (3) School strike — Trump denies US responsibility despite footage showing Tomahawk (US-only munition), now says "under investigation"; (4) War duration — Campaign promised "no new wars," initially projected 4-5 weeks, now says "way ahead of schedule" and "very close to finishing," but Hegseth said "just the beginning" one day earlier. Peace deal was "within reach" per Oman mediator before strikes began.

## Key contradictions documented

### Regime change
- **Feb 28:** Trump urged Iranians to "seize control" of destiny and overthrow clerical rule
- **Mar 2:** Hegseth: "This is not a so-called regime-change war... laser-focused" on missiles, navy, nuclear
- **Mar 5:** Trump to Axios: "I have to be involved in the appointment" of Iran's new leader
- **Florida presser:** Trump says he prefers "internal" leader, references Venezuela formula (US-backed Delcy Rodríguez after Maduro kidnapping)
- On Mojtaba as target: "inappropriate to say"

### Pre-emptive self-defense justification
- **Day 1:** UN Ambassador Waltz invoked Article 51 (self-defense)
- **Day 3:** Rubio said US knew Israel would strike first, so launched preemptively against "imminent threat" of Iran retaliation
- **Day 4:** Trump contradicted Rubio: "I might have forced Israel's hand"
- Context: Peace deal was "within our reach" per Oman mediator; Iran had agreed to never stockpile nuclear material for bomb

### School strike
- Trump repeatedly denied US responsibility: "In my opinion... that was done by Iran" (Mar 7)
- Footage shows Tomahawk cruise missile — only US possesses in this war (AP, CBS, BBC analysis)
- Florida presser: Trump claims other countries "might also have Tomahawks, including Iran"
- Softened: "under investigation... willing to live with that report"

### War duration
- Campaign promise: "no new wars"
- Hegseth (Mar 2): "This is not Iraq"
- Trump initially: 4-5 weeks
- Trump (Mar 9 CBS): "I think the war is very complete"
- Trump (Florida): "short-term excursion," "very close to finishing," "very soon"
- Asked if ending this week: "No, but I think soon"
- **Hegseth (Mar 9):** "This is only just the beginning"
