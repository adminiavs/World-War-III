# Trump says Iran war very complete, sending markets surging and oil diving

**Date:** 2026-03-10
**Source:** Capital Brief
**Author:** Paulina Durán
**URL:** https://www.capitalbrief.com/briefing/trump-says-iran-war-very-complete-sending-markets-surging-and-oil-diving-53465825-c6ea-4c3e-aa15-7f33aae79f66/
**Tags:** trump, cbs, mojtaba, hormuz, oil, markets

## tl;dr

Trump told CBS (phone from Doral golf club) war with Iran was "very complete, pretty much"; Iran had "nothing left in a military sense"; US "very far ahead of schedule" vs initial ~one month estimate. "They've no navy, no communications, they've got no air force. Their missiles are down to a scatter." No message for Mojtaba Khamenei; has someone else in mind to lead Iran. On Hormuz: ships have been entering; still "thinking about taking it over"; any further Iranian aggression would be "the end of that country." Published after Dept of War posted "We have Only Just Begun to Fight" and "no mercy" on X. **Markets:** Bloomberg headline "Trump Says War Could Be Over Soon"; S&P 500 and Nasdaq both surged >1% after earlier falling up to 1.5%; WTI dropped below $86 (had traded above $119); US 10y yield fell 4bp to 4.10%; dollar index dipped 0.2%.
