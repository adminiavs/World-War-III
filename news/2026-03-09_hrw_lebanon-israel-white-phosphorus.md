# Lebanon: Israel Unlawfully Using White Phosphorus

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** Human Rights Watch
**URL:** https://www.hrw.org/news/2026/03/09/lebanon-israel-unlawfully-using-white-phosphorus
**Tags:** lebanon, israel, white-phosphorus, yohmor, incendiary-weapons, ihl

## tl;dr

HRW verified and geolocated seven images showing Israeli artillery-fired white phosphorus munitions airburst over the southern Lebanese town of Yohmor on 3 March 2026, over residential areas. Civil defence responded to fires in at least two homes and one car. Under IHL, airburst white phosphorus in populated areas is unlawfully indiscriminate. Israel had ordered Yohmor and 50 other villages to evacuate that morning; HRW could not verify whether anyone was present or injured. HRW previously documented Israeli white phosphorus use in southern Lebanon Oct 2023–May 2024. M825-series 155mm projectile identified. HRW calls on Israel to prohibit airburst white phosphorus in populated areas; urges US, UK, Germany to suspend military assistance and arms sales to Israel; urges Lebanon to accede to ICC Rome Statute. Israel is not party to Protocol III (incendiary weapons); Lebanon is.
