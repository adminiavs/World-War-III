## tl;dr

Australian energy stocks surging as Iran strikes oil and gas infrastructure across the Gulf. War is a boon for Australian energy producers (gas exporters benefit from higher LNG prices) while a burden for everyone else (consumers, manufacturers, farmers). Highlights the paradox of energy-exporting nations in wartime.
