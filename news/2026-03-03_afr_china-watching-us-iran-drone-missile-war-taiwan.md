## tl;dr

China is closely watching the US-Iran drone and missile war as a real-time intelligence and technology showcase for the PLA. Despite China's energy, trade and investment ties with Iran, there is little evidence Beijing is supplying Tehran with the drones and missiles used in its counterattack. ---

## Full story

Despite China's broad energy, trade and investment alliance with Iran, there is little evidence that Beijing is supplying Tehran with the drones and missiles at the centre of its counterattack against the US and Israel.

Reports that China is shipping air defence systems and missile propellant ingredients to Iran have been around since late last year, though neither side has commented.

The US campaign in Iran is becoming a real-time intelligence feed and technology show for the People's Liberation Army. Analysts and regional security sources suggest Beijing is watching for lessons on US and allied defences, strike patterns, and escalation dynamics, with implications for a potential Taiwan scenario.

Jessica Sier, North Asia correspondent. Mar 4, 2026.
