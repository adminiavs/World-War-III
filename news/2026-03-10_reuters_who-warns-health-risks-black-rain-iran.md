# WHO warns health risks from black rain in Iran

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Source:** Reuters  
**URL:** https://www.reuters.com/sustainability/society-equity/who-warns-health-risks-black-rain-iran-2026-03-10/  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** Iran / global  
**Tags:** who, health, black-rain, pollution, tehran

## tl;dr

WHO warns of health risks from black rain and pollution in Iran following strikes on fuel depots and industrial sites. Iranian fuel depots hit so hard oil rained from sky in Tehran; air quality severely degraded. Toxic ash and combustion products; respiratory and skin hazards; contamination of water and soil. Scientists report unprecedented pollution; acid rain risk.
