# Iran Makes Threat Against Trump

## Source
- TIME (10 Mar 2026)

## Key Content

Ali Larijani (head of Iranian National Security Council) on X: Iran does not fear your empty threats. Even those bigger than you could not eliminate Iran. Be careful not to get eliminated yourself.

Response to Trump TruthSocial post threatening to hit Iran twenty times harder if oil shipment not resumed through Strait of Hormuz.

Larijani has led Iranian retaliatory strikes since war began. After Khamenei assassination, he vowed to hold Trump responsible.
