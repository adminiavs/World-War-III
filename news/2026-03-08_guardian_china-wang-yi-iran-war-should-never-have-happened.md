# China's foreign minister says Iran war 'should never have happened'

**Date:** 2026-03-08  
**Source:** The Guardian  
**Author:** Amy Hawkins (Beijing)  
**URL:** https://www.theguardian.com/world/2026/mar/08/chinas-foreign-minister-says-iran-war-should-never-have-happened  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** China, US, Iran  
**Tags:** china, wang-yi, diplomacy, trump-xi, regime-change

## tl;dr

Wang Yi at Two Sessions: Middle East war "should never have happened"; regime change "will find no popular support"; "strong fist does not mean strong reason"; world cannot return to "law of the jungle." Notably avoided directly criticising the US; stressed "mutual respect" and that Trump–Xi talks were "heartening." 2026 a "big year" for China–US relations; neither side can "remodel the other." Markedly softer than last year (no "two-faced" tariff attack). Trump visit to China expected 31 Mar–2 Apr; both sides focused on keeping relationship stable ahead of trip despite Venezuela capture and Iran war.
