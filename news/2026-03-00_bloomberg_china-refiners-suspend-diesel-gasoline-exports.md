## Full story

5 March 2026: China government ordered major refiners to halt diesel and gasoline exports. NDRC instructed stop new export contracts and negotiate cancellations; to prepare for prolonged Iran crisis and secure domestic fuel. China about 44% imported oil from Middle East; Gulf conflict disrupting crude. Halts apply to diesel and gasoline; jet fuel for international flights, bunkering, HK/Macau excluded. Diesel margins near $49/bbl (three-year highs). Zhejiang Petrochemical and Sinopec Fujian among refineries cutting throughput. India, Japan, Indonesia also cutting runs. Bloomberg fetch timed out.
