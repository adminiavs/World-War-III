# Scoop: U.S. dismayed by Israel Iran fuel strikes

**Date:** 2026-03-08  
**Source:** Axios  
**URL:** https://www.axios.com/2026/03/08/us-dismayed-israel-iran-fuel-strikes  
**Authors:** Marc Caputo, Barak Ravid  
**Tags:** us-israel-tensions, fuel-depots, tehran-strikes, oil-prices, qalibaf, 200-oil

## tl;dr

Israel struck 30 Iranian fuel depots Saturday; scope went beyond what the U.S. expected. First significant US-Israel disagreement since war began. U.S. worried strikes could backfire and push oil higher. Israeli source: U.S. message was WTF. Trump adviser: president does not like the attack, wants to save the oil. Qalibaf warned Iran could retaliate and oil could hit $200/bbl.
