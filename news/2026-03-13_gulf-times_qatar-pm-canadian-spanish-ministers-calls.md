# Qatar PM receives phone calls from Canadian, Spanish ministers

**Date:** 2026-03-13  
**Source:** Gulf Times (QNA)  
**URL:** https://www.gulf-times.com/article/722328/qatar/pm-receives-phone-calls-from-canadian-spanish-ministers/amp  
**Region:** Qatar, Canada, Spain  

---

## tl;dr

Qatar PM and Foreign Minister Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman bin Jassim Al Thani received phone calls Thursday 13 March from Canada's Minister of Foreign Affairs Anita Anand and Spain's Minister of Foreign Affairs Jose Manuel Alvarez. Discussions covered military escalation in the region, repercussions on regional and international security, and ways to resolve disputes peacefully. PM reiterated condemnation of Iranian attacks on Qatar as "unacceptable under any pretext"; noted Qatar has sought to distance itself from regional conflicts and facilitate dialogue between Iran and the international community. Both Anand and Alvarez called for de-escalation, application of reason, and return to negotiations and diplomatic means to avoid further chaos.
