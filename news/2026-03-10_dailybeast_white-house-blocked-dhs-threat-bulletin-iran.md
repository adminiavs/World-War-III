# Trump administration blocked DHS threat bulletin on Iran-related homeland risk

**Date:** 2026-03-07 / 09  
**Source:** The Daily Beast  
**URL:** https://www.thedailybeast.com/trump-goons-ordered-to-hide-major-war-threat-from-americans/  
**Author:** Julia Ornedo  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** US  
**Tags:** dhs, fbi, nctc, threat-bulletin, white-house, homeland-security

## tl;dr

FBI, DHS, National Counterterrorism Center were preparing five-page joint bulletin "Elevated threat in the United States during US-Iran conflict" warning radicalized individuals may see conflict as justification for violence; details on Iranian proxy attack methods. DHS gave White House heads-up; top officials ordered it placed on "hold." DHS source: "any unclassified FOUO information going forward concerning Iran has to be reviewed by the White House before any dissemination." "Chilling effect on keeping state and local law enforcement informed." Against FBI leadership wishes.
