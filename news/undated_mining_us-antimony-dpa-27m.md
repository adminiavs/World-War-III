# US Antimony Receives $27M Defense Production Act Funding

**Date:** 2026-03-00  
**Source:** MINING.COM  
**URL:** https://www.mining.com/us-antimony-receives-27m-defense-production-act-funding/

## tl;dr

US Department of War invested $27M in United States Antimony under Trump critical minerals strategy. Funds for domestic antimony extraction, processing, refinement; antimony is critical for munitions and defense. ---

## Full story

The US Defense Department (Department of War) has made a $27 million investment into United States Antimony (UAMY) in alignment with the Trump administration critical minerals strategy. The funds would allow the company to enhance domestic extraction, processing, and refinement of antimony. The US government considers antimony critical to economic and national security. US Antimony is the only fully integrated producer outside China and Russia, operating a large smelting facility in Montana. Assistant Secretary of War for Industrial Base Policy Mike Cadenazzi: This investment will address risk in one of our most critical munitions and materials supply chains. The investment comes from DPA Title III funds, part of the Ukraine Supplemental Appropriations Act of 2022; one of three DPA Purchases Office investments since start of fiscal 2026. Deployment was delayed by the US government shutdown. MINING.COM.
