# Australia Deploys Military Planes to Evacuate Citizens from Middle East Amid Rising Tensions

**Date:** 2026-03-05  
**Source:** WION  
**URL:** https://www.wionews.com/videos/australia-deploys-military-planes-to-evacuate-citizens-from-middle-east-amid-rising-tensions-1772704758707

## tl;dr

Prime Minister Anthony Albanese confirmed that two Australian military planes have been deployed to the Middle East to assist Australians stranded amid escalating regional tensions. The aircraft are tasked with evacuating citizens safely and ensuring they return home without incident. Defence officials emphasise the operation demonstrates Australia's commitment to protecting its nationals abroad while supporting stability in the region.

## Full story

Australia's Prime Minister Anthony Albanese confirmed that two Australian military planes have been deployed to the Middle East to assist Australians stranded amid escalating regional tensions. The aircraft are tasked with evacuating citizens safely and ensuring they return home without incident. Defence officials emphasize the operation demonstrates Australia's commitment to protecting its nationals abroad while supporting stability in the region.
