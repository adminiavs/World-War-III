## Full story

With US regime change operations in Venezuela and Iran, UBP asks if US focus elsewhere gives China an opportunity or incentive to move against Taiwan. US is stretched; USS Abraham Lincoln moved from East Asia to Middle East. UBP sees two constraints: US has rebuilt ally capabilities (Japan, Australia, Philippines; Japan F-35s and light carriers; Philippines bases). Iran may offer China a test of its equipment; China had reportedly delivered kit to Iran; outcome (not deployed or ineffective) affects Taiwan risk-reward. China may prefer buying time, depleting US stocks, and seeing equipment proven in Iran; supplying Iran while constraining US restock could make Iran a quagmire. Watch: Xi-Trump summit end March, suspended Taiwan arms deal, Japan defence spending. UBP 4 Mar 2026, Norman Villamin.
