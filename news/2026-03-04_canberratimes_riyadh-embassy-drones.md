---

## Full story

The US embassy in Riyadh has been hit by two drones resulting in a limited fire and some material damage. The drone attack came amid ongoing Iranian missile and drone strikes on Gulf states that host US bases, following US and Israeli strikes on Iran on Saturday. The US embassy told its citizens to shelter in place immediately. It also issued shelter in place for Jeddah and Dhahran and limited non-essential travel to military installations. Saudi Ministry of Defence said on X that a limited fire broke out at the embassy, causing minor material damage. No reported injuries; building was empty in the early morning. The Canberra Times, 4 Mar 2026. AAP.
