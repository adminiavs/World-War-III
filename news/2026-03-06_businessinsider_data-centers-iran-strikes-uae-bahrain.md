## Full story

Three Amazon data centers in the Middle East (two UAE, one Bahrain) were damaged by drone strikes; AWS evacuated staff and closed one site due to structural damage and flooding. Iran state media said IRGC deliberately targeted Amazon for supporting US military activities. First instance of Big Tech data centers hit in military operations. Middle East has about 4.5 GW data center capacity with more planned; CSIS (James Lewis) said data centers are the new critical infrastructure and defence is now a question. Careem and banking apps saw disruptions. Amazon urged customers to migrate workloads from the region. FT and Futurism also reported; Iran reportedly targeted Microsoft site. Business Insider 6 Mar 2026, Hugh Langley.
