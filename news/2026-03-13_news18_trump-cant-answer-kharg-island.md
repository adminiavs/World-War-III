# Trump says ‘can’t answer’ question on possible US move to occupy Iran’s Kharg Island

**Date:** 13 March 2026
**Source:** News18
**URL:** https://www.news18.com/world/trump-says-cant-answer-question-on-possible-us-move-to-occupy-irans-kharg-island-ws-l-9959573.html

---

## tl;dr

In a radio interview with Brian Kilmeade, Trump declined to answer whether the US could consider occupying Kharg Island (handles ~90% of Iran’s oil exports). “I can’t answer a question like that.” Said such a move was “not high on the list” but “it’s one of so many different things, and I can change my mind in seconds.” Criticised the question as inappropriate and “foolish” — “What would I tell you? ‘Oh, yes, Brian, I’m thinking about doing it, let me let you know what time and when it’ll take place.’” Kharg is ~25 km off Bushehr; Iran’s main oil export terminal.

**Impact on predictions:** GROUND-TROOPS-TRACKER and Jiang both flag Kharg as a possible first physical occupation. Trump’s non-denial keeps the option live; “can change my mind in seconds” matches his earlier phrasing. Watch for MEU/amphibious use if Kharg moves up the list.
