# Israeli forces kill Palestinian couple and two of their children in occupied West Bank

**Date:** 2026-03-15  
**Source:** BBC News (Jo Floto)  
**URL:** https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/cqxdndz75zvo

---

## tl;dr

Israeli forces shot and killed four members of the Bani Odeh family in their car in Tammum, northern West Bank: parents Ali and Waad, and two children Mohammed (5) and Othman (7). Two older children (8 and 11) survived with minor shrapnel injuries. Israeli military and border police said the car accelerated toward forces who felt endangered and fired; circumstances under investigation. Palestinian health ministry said all four had gunshot wounds to face and head. Palestinian Red Crescent said Israeli forces initially prevented crews from reaching the injured. UN: much of West Bank under heightened movement restrictions since US–Israel–Iran war began 28 Feb; OCHA: 1,064 Palestinians killed in West Bank since 7 Oct 2023, including at least 231 children.

## Full story

A Palestinian husband and wife and two of their young children were shot and killed by Israeli forces in the occupied West Bank. The Bani Odeh family were travelling in their car in the town of Tammum when Israeli forces opened fire. The Palestinian health ministry identified the dead as parents Ali and Waad and children Mohammed (5) and Othman (7), all with gunshot wounds to the face and head. Two older children, aged eight and 11, survived with minor shrapnel injuries. Israeli authorities said a joint army and border police operation was under way and the car accelerated toward forces who responded with fire; circumstances are being investigated. The Palestinian Red Crescent said Israeli forces initially prevented medics from reaching the injured. The UN says much of the West Bank has remained under heightened movement restrictions since the outbreak of the war between the US, Israel and Iran on 28 February. OCHA reported 1,064 Palestinians killed in the occupied West Bank between 7 October 2023 and 8 March 2026, including at least 231 children.
