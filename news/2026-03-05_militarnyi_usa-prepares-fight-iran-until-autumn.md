## Full story

The USA is preparing to conduct military operations against Iran for at least 100 days. Potentially until September, according to Politico, citing an internal Pentagon document. This indicates the seriousness and scale of the planned actions in the region.

The State Department is allocating additional resources to evacuate Americans from the Middle East, and the Pentagon is urgently increasing the number of troops engaged in reconnaissance for the operation.

According to the publication, this mobilization indicates that the US administration was not fully prepared for the larger-scale war it has had to face.

The current situation demonstrates growing tensions between the US and Iran, as well as Washington's readiness for a prolonged military presence in the region.

At the same time, the US plans to allocate an additional $50 billion to replenish the stockpile of weapons used against Iran.
