## tl;dr

Max Hastings argues the US-Israel war on Iran has armed Netanyahu to impose almost any sentence on the Palestinians; Palestinian statehood is dead. Netanyahu was on the ropes two years ago (corruption, blamed for Oct 2023 security failures); now rides high, wants Israeli election advanced to June, has seduced Trump into partnership attacking Iran. Poll: 93% of Jewish Israelis support the war. Lapid and Bennett: no coalition and no opposition, only one people and one IDF. Israeli finance minister Smotrich declared government purpose in tightening grip on West Bank was to kill the idea of a Palestinian state. Settlement expansion and road-building will cut West Bank in half. US administrations once imposed restraints; today acquiescent silence in Washington. Palestinian Authority a zombie. Hastings: Trump has empowered Netanyahu to annex the West Bank, forge Greater Israel; nobody likely to stop him. Palestinians have no friends and no leaders who command respect. Best hope for Palestinians may be diaspora. Occupation has made us a cruel people (former Shin Bet head, The Gatekeepers). Opens with Jad Jadallah, 14, shot in West Bank; 55 children killed there by Israeli forces last year (UN); settler violence rarely punished.
