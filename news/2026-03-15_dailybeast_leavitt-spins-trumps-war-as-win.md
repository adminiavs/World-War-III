## Full story

White House Press Secretary Karoline Leavitt, speaking on Fox News to Lara Trump, said the White House is working 24/7 to make sure Americans understand how much of a tremendous success the operation has been. She said she spent nearly an hour on the phone with CNN rebutting a story that the US was unprepared for Iran to blockade the Strait of Hormuz; the Wall Street Journal reported that General Dan Caine warned Trump multiple times of that risk and Trump went ahead. The Pentagon has formally identified the six service members killed in the KC-135 crash. Leavitt said that if you picked up The New York Times you would think Iran is crushing the United States but it is quite the opposite. She said the fact that terrorists are holding the global oil industry hostage by threatening the strait underscores the need for President Trump to launch this operation and that he has a plethora of solutions. Trump's stated objectives have shifted from decapitation and regime change to potential boots on the ground and the war ending when he can feel it in his bones. Polls show a majority of Americans disapprove. On Truth Social Trump claimed the US has beaten and completely decimated Iran while also posting that Iran could still send a drone or two or drop a mine in the waterway.
