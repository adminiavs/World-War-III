## Full article

Donald Trump has deployed 5,000 extra troops to the Strait of Hormuz as Washington looks to regain control of the world's oil supply. Secretary of Defense Hegseth approved a request by US Central Command for a Marine expeditionary unit.

**Kharg Island:** The Trump administration is debating whether to seize Iran's Kharg Island, ~16 miles off the mainland, which handles 90% of Iran's fuel exports. Trump: "It's not high on the list, but it's one of so many different things, and I can change my mind in seconds."

**Hegseth** at Pentagon briefing: "You don't need to worry about it." He refused to answer questions on why America was not able to defend the Strait.

Gas prices reached $3.60/gallon in the US, up from $2.90 before the war.

**Iran's security chief Ali Larijani** (attending Iran's most high-profile public event since Khamenei's death at a pro-regime march in Tehran): "These attacks are out of fear, out of desperation. One who is strong wouldn't bomb demonstrations at all. It's clear that it has failed." On Trump: "He doesn't understand that the Iranian people are a brave nation, a strong nation, a determined nation. The more he presses, the stronger the nation's determination will become." Larijani said Trump would be "sorry" for his "grave miscalculation."

**Mojtaba Khamenei:** Reportedly in a coma after losing one or both legs and sustaining serious stomach or liver damage (The Sun). Has not been seen since becoming Supreme Leader.

**Trump's Truth Social post:** "We are totally destroying the terrorist regime of Iran, militarily economically, and otherwise... We have unparalleled firepower, unlimited ammunition, and plenty of time. They've been killing innocent people all over the world for 47 years, and now I, as the 47th President of the United States of America, am killing them. What a great honor it is to do so!"
