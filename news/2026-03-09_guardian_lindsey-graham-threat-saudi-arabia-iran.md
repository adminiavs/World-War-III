# Trump ally Lindsey Graham issues threat to Saudi Arabia over Iran war

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** The Guardian
**Author:** Joseph Gedeon
**URL:** https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2026/mar/09/lindsey-graham-saudi-arabia-iran
**Tags:** graham, saudi-arabia, gulf, defence-pact, wsj

## tl;dr

Senator Lindsey Graham questioned whether US should honour a long-sought defence agreement with Saudi Arabia; kingdom's refusal to join military operations against Iran made partnership "difficult to justify" when Americans are dying and US spending billions. X post: US embassy Riyadh being evacuated due to Iranian attacks; frustration Riyadh declined to participate militarily despite shared interest defeating Iran. "Americans are dying... Meanwhile Saudi Arabia seems to be issuing statements and doing things in the background that are marginally helpful." Hoped GCC would "get more involved"; "If not, consequences will follow." Riyadh seeks formal US security guarantee (Japan-style); $142bn arms package May 2025; major non-Nato ally Nov 2025; binding defence pact unsigned. Saudi FM earlier Monday condemned Iranian strikes, retained right to safeguard security; no mention of joining US operations. WSJ: Graham spent months pressing Trump to authorize strikes; raised idea during golf after 2024 election; trips to Israel; spoke with MBS; advised Netanyahu on approaching Trump; worked with Jack Keane, Marc Thiessen. Casualties cited: seven US military personnel, eight wounded; ~1,255 killed in Iran (health minister); 397 Lebanon; 11 Israel; several Gulf including four UAE. State Dept: 36,000+ Americans returned from Middle East since 28 Feb.
