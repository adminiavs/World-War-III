## tl;dr

Trump wore a baseball cap during the dignified transfer for six US soldiers killed in the Iran conflict; did not remove it or bow his head. Viral reaction: "no respect," "what a loser," "entirely inappropriate," "needs all the attention on himself." Removing hat is traditional sign of respect; 193rd SOW / military etiquette guidance cited — civilians should remove headgear and place over heart. Trump and Vance present with spouses, Hegseth and officials. Trump later called it "a very sad day," said deceased were "great people, great parents." Lists the six killed: O'Brien, Khork, Marzan, Amor, Tietjens, Coady.
