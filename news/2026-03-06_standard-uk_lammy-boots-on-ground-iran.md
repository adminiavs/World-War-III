## Full story

Britain says America will need to put boots on the ground in Iran if Donald Trump wants to choose the next Supreme Leader. Deputy Prime Minister David Lammy also warned Iran could end up with a more hardline leader than Khamenei. UK has refused to join Trump's war; a government charter from Muscat landed at Stansted; UK is repatriating possibly more than 140,000 citizens from the Gulf. Lammy told Sky and BBC that regime change has not been succeeded from the air anywhere; the successor will be for the Iranian people unless there is boots on the ground. Trump attacked Starmer for refusing UK bases for offensive strikes. Evening Standard, Nicholas Cecil.
