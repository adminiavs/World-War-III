# Iran IRGC responds to Trump: we will determine end of war

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Source:** News18  
**URL:** https://www.news18.com/amp/world/irans-irgc-responds-to-trump-says-it-will-be-the-one-who-will-determine-the-end-of-war-9951631.html  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** Iran  
**Tags:** irgc, trump, hormuz, oil

## tl;dr

IRGC: "It is we who will determine the end of the war. The equations and future status of the region are now in the hands of our armed forces; American forces will not end the war." Response to Trump saying conflict could end soon. Trump: considering taking Strait of Hormuz; if Iran blocks oil, "end of that country." IRGC also warned no oil would leave region if strikes continue. Trump told CBS: Iran has no navy, air force, radar, telecoms, leadership; "nothing left in a military sense."
