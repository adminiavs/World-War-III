# War expands to central Beirut as Israeli strike kills Iranians in Ramada hotel

**Date:** 2026-03-08  
**Source:** BBC  
**URL:** https://www.bbc.com/news/articles/c2k8y7vy78no  
**Author:** Alice Cuddy  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** Lebanon  
**Tags:** beirut, ramada, israel, quds-force, hezbollah

## tl;dr

Israeli strike on Ramada Plaza hotel in Raouche (central Beirut) ~0130; first strike in city center. IDF: killed five Quds Force operatives (Majid Hassani funds to Hezbollah; Alireza Bi-Azar, Ahmad Rasouli intel; Hossein Ahmadlou, Abu Mohammad Ali). Iran UN mission: four diplomats (second secretary, third secretary, attaché); "cowardly terrorist assassination." Lebanese health: 4 killed, 10 injured; bystanders among injured. Three Lebanese booked rooms used by targets; hotel hit three times, two munitions did not explode. IDF warned Iranian govt reps to leave Lebanon; 150+ Iranians left. Displaced families in hotel; children injured by shrapnel. War expanding; "no safe place in Lebanon."
