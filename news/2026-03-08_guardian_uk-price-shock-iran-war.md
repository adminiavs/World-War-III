# UK must be prepared for a price shock from the Iran war

**Date:** 2026-03-08  
**Source:** The Guardian  
**Author:** Heather Stewart  
**URL:** https://www.theguardian.com/business/2026/mar/08/uk-must-be-prepared-for-a-price-shock-from-the-iran-war  
**Content type:** opinion  
**Region/country:** UK  
**Tags:** inflation, oil, energy, price-cap, bank-of-england

## tl;dr

UK must brace for another price shock from Trump's Iran war. RAC: conflict has already added 3p/litre to unleaded. If gas rise is sustained, July price cap could push household bills up sharply. Oil shocks are especially painful (fertiliser, transport, manufacturing); poorer households hit hardest. Central banks may "look through" supply shocks but MPC likely to hold off rate cuts. Governments may need to secure supplies, protect poorest, crack down on price gouging; long-term answer is "homegrown" clean power (Miliband).
