## Full story

Live coverage of the Iran conflict. Key updates: An Iranian warship has docked in Sri Lanka days after the US sank an Iranian frigate; Sri Lanka evacuated 208 crew from the vessel (IRINS Bushehr) after an emergency request to dock. Two Australian sailors were reportedly onboard the US submarine that sank the Iranian frigate as part of an AUKUS training rotation; Foreign Minister Penny Wong refused to confirm or deny in Question Time ("for operational and security reasons we don't disclose specific information regarding Australian personnel"). The Iranian frigate (IRIS Dena) was attacked Wednesday with at least 87 killed; Pentagon released video of the attack; submarine identified in report as USS Charlotte, Mark 48 torpedo. Israel Defence Minister Israel Katz told N12 TV that Israel had planned to kill Khamenei in November in a "very tight forum" with the prime minister; the plan was brought to the US and brought forward after protests in Iran. Iranian Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi said Iran is ready for a US ground invasion: "We are waiting for them … We are confident that we can confront them, and that would be a big disaster for them." He refused negotiations and said Iran had not asked for a ceasefire. Trump called Mojtaba Khamenei a "lightweight" and said he wanted to be involved in choosing Iran's next leader. Prime Minister Albanese confirmed military assets (two aircraft) deployed to the Middle East for repatriation. Israelis set up a memorial on the Tel Aviv boardwalk for six US service members killed in an Iranian drone attack in Kuwait. France will provide Lebanon with armoured transport vehicles and operational and logistical support; Macron urged Hezbollah to cease fire and Israel to refrain from ground intervention. Iran denied launching drones at Azerbaijan's airport (suggested false-flag by Israel); Azerbaijani President Aliyev called the strikes "an act of terror" and "ugly, cowardly, and shameless" and said armed forces would prepare retaliatory measures. Slain leader Khamenei laid to rest in holy Mashhad after state funeral.
