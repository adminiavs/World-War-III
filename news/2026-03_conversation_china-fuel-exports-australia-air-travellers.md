# Chinas ban on fuel exports deeply worrying for Australian air travellers

**Date:** March 2026
**Source:** The Conversation
**URL:** https://theconversation.com/chinas-ban-on-fuel-exports-is-deeply-worrying-for-australian-air-travellers-278313

## tl;dr

Australia imports ~32 pct jet fuel from China; Sydney has no refinery capacity. ~29-32 days jet fuel reserve; not meeting IEA 90-day. If deliveries stop reserves run low in weeks; rationing and flight cuts likely. Air NZ already cut 1100 flights. Other Asian suppliers may restrict exports.

**Impact:** Confirms oil-to-jet-fuel crisis chain; Australia guide China jet fuel section.
