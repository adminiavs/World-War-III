## Full story

Defense Secretary Pete Hegseth stated the U.S. has no shortage of munitions and that stockpiles allow Operation Epic Fury to be sustained as long as needed. Mission: destroy Iran's offensive missile capabilities, cripple the Iranian navy, and prevent Iran from obtaining nuclear weapons. Congress surged $25 billion into munition procurement (2025 reconciliation); Lockheed Martin reached a deal with the Pentagon in January to accelerate missile development. Iranian missile attacks decreased 90% since day one, drone attacks 83%. Operation cost estimated about $891 million per day (first four days roughly $3.7 billion).
