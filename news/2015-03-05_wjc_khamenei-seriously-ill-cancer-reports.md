# Reports: Iranian supreme leader seriously ill with prostate cancer

**Date:** 2015-03-05  
**Source:** World Jewish Congress  
**URL:** https://www.worldjewishcongress.org/en/news/iranian-supreme-leader-seriously-ill-with-cancer-say-reports  
**Tags:** khamenei, health, prostate-cancer, historical, 2015

## tl;dr

Historical report (March 2015): Western intelligence and media reported Ayatollah Ali Khamenei in serious condition in a Tehran hospital with prostate cancer that had reportedly spread. Le Figaro cited officials saying the cancer was discovered about ten years earlier, stage four, and doctors estimated he had two years left to live. He had undergone surgery in September 2014; official Iranian news said it had been successful. In Iran his condition was not public. Khamenei was 75; had ruled since 1989; survived a 1981 assassination attempt that left his right arm paralyzed.
