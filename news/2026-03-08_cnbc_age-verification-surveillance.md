# Online age-verification tools surveilling adults

**Date:** 2026-03-08  
**Source:** CNBC  
**URL:** https://www.cnbc.com/2026/03/08/social-media-child-safety-internet-ai-surveillance.html  
**Tags:** age-verification, surveillance, privacy, virginia

## tl;dr

US child safety laws pull millions of adults into mandatory age-verification gates using AI. Half of states have or are advancing such laws. Virginia court blocked state law in First Amendment challenge. EFF warns age verification strikes at free and open internet.
