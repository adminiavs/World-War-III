# Marine deployment reeks of desperation from Trump

**Date:** 14 March 2026
**Source:** Australian Financial Review
**URL:** https://www.afr.com/world/middle-east/marine-deployment-reeks-of-desperation-from-trump-20260314-p5oafu

---

## tl;dr

AFR analysis: the deployment of 5,000 Marines two weeks into combat -- with a further two weeks before they arrive -- "reinforces how badly Trump and his war planners misjudged their Iranian foes." It is "not so much of a gamble but reeks more of desperation to get back on the front foot." The deployment risks sparking the very thing Trump is desperate to avoid: a ground war. The president wants a quick exit but needs the Strait of Hormuz reopened first.

---

## Key quotes

Andrew Tillett (AFR Europe correspondent, London):

"Donald Trump's deployment of up to 5000 Marines to the Middle East marks a significant escalation and risks sparking what the president is desperate to avoid: a ground war involving US troops."

"For the troop movement to take place two weeks into combat -- and it will be a fortnight before they arrive, reportedly -- reinforces how badly Trump and his war planners misjudged their Iranian foes."

"It's not so much of a gamble but reeks more of desperation to get back on the front foot."
