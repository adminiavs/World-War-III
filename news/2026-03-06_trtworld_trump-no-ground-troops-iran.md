## Full story

President Trump said Thursday it would be a waste of time currently to consider sending US ground troops into Iran (NBC). He dismissed Foreign Minister Araghchi's warning that such a move would spell disaster. They have lost everything, their navy, everything they can lose. Trump said he wants to go in and clean out everything and does not want someone who would rebuild over 10 years; he has ideas for a new leader. He had said he would have to be involved in choosing Iran's next leader after Khamenei was killed. Heavy attacks were reported in Tehran Friday. TRT World and agencies.
