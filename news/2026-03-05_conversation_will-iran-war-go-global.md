## Full story

Before the first strike analysts warned war on Tehran would be highly risky. Iran has a huge military, proxies and stockpiles. Iran has responded by attacking Israel and US targets in UAE, Bahrain, Saudi, Kuwait; attacks from Cyprus, Iraq, Jordan; Hezbollah has joined; Beirut bombarded. Economic damage: oil refineries shut, Strait of Hormuz effectively closed, evacuations, travel cancelled. Killing Khamenei has not collapsed the regime; smart money on son Mojtaba. A week ago US and Iranian negotiators were in Geneva with "significant progress"; now escalation is unpredictable. US submarine sank Iranian warship off Sri Lanka. YouGov/Economist (March 2): US oppose 45% to 32%; Independents against; bad for Republican midterms. NBC: administration may invoke Defense Production Act for munitions. Starmer said UK would not join (lawful basis); has since softened. Trump unimpressed; "special relationship" under strain. China condemned; dependent on Iranian oil. Putin watched Khamenei assassination with alarm; short term war may play to Moscow as munitions diverted from Ukraine.
