# Australian Army 1st Division prepares for Middle East despite Wong no-combat promise

**Date:** 2026-03-09  
**Source:** The Nightly  
**URL:** https://thenightly.com.au/politics/iran-war-australian-armys-1st-division-prepares-for-middle-east-despite-penny-wongs-no-combat-promise--c-21883920  
**Tags:** Iran war, Australia, Army, 1st Division, Penny Wong, deployment, Middle East

## tl;dr

Confidential source: Army 1st Division (Brisbane, Maj Gen Collingburn) has begun planning for possible Middle East deployment; unclear if combat, support or peacekeeping. Wong has ruled out ground troops in Iran; Australia already contributed (three personnel on US sub that sank Dena). Joe Hockey (Sky): "Boots on the ground" expected soon. ADA's Neil James: contingency planning would be normal. Air Force (F-35, Wedgetail) and Navy (destroyer/frigate) seen more likely than Army for Gulf role.
