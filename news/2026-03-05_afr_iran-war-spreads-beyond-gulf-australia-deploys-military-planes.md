## Full story

The Iran war is spilling beyond the Middle East, with a US submarine sinking an Iranian warship off Sri Lanka, NATO air defences intercepting a missile as it approached Turkey and Tehran threatening to strike Israel's nuclear reactor and Israeli embassies around the world. As Kurdish officials said they were prepared to cross into western Iran to mount a ground threat to the Islamic Republic, strikes continued across the Middle East, although US officials said the rate of Iranian missile launches was declining after the successful targeting of mobile launchers. (AFR, Mar 5 2026; London/Canberra.)
