# Trump invokes defense law on phosphorus, herbicide supplies

**Date:** 2026-02-18  
**Source:** Bloomberg  
**URL:** https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-02-18/trump-invokes-defense-law-on-phosphorus-herbicide-supplies  
**Tags:** trump, defense-production-act, phosphorus, fertilizer, herbicide, supply-chain

## tl;dr

President Trump invoked the Defense Production Act or similar defense authorities regarding phosphorus and herbicide supplies. The move is linked to securing inputs critical for agriculture (fertilizer) and possibly industrial supply chains ahead of or during the Iran crisis—phosphorus is a key fertilizer component and the Strait of Hormuz region is a major supplier of sulphur and urea.
