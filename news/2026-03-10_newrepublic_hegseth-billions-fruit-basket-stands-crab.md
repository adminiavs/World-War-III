## tl;dr

Open the Books: Pentagon spent $93B in Sept 2025 (end of FY) — highest since 2008. Frivolous purchases under Hegseth: Steinway grand piano $98K (Air Force chief of staff home), $5.3M Apple devices, $2M Alaskan king crab, $6.9M lobster tail, $15.1M ribeye, $225M furniture (fruit basket stands $12K, Herman Miller recliners $60K+). Last five days of Sept: $50.1B grants/contracts — more than Canada+Mexico military budgets. Use-it-or-lose-it year-end spending. Weeks later millions lost SNAP amid shutdown.
