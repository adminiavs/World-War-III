## Full story

Sinking of Iranian warship off Sri Lanka is the first time since WWII an American submarine has attacked a surface vessel; dramatic widening of combat in the five-day war. IRIS Dena sunk by US submarine torpedo; Sri Lanka recovered 87 bodies, rescued 32. Hegseth said US sunk an Iranian vessel that believed it was safe in international waters. Ship hit by huge underwater explosion; US boats typically use Mark 48 heavyweight torpedoes. Strike by US nuclear attack sub. Dena was 3000 km from Iran, about 30 hours from Diego Garcia. US also struck Shahid Bagheri and sank Soleimani; at least 20 Iranian naval vessels sunk (Admiral Cooper). Dena was returning from MILAN exercise off India; launched 2015, Moudge-class, sanctioned 2023. Hegseth claim of first sub sink since WWII incorrect; Belgrano 1982, others. SMH, 5 Mar 2026. Tom Housden. AP, Bloomberg.
