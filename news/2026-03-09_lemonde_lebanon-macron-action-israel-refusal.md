# Lebanon: Macron calls for action but faces Israel's refusal

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** Le Monde
**Authors:** Claire Gatinois, Hélène Sallon
**URL:** https://www.lemonde.fr/en/international/article/2026/03/09/lebanon-macron-calls-for-action-but-faces-israel-s-refusal_6751262_4.html
**Tags:** france, macron, lebanon, hezbollah, netanyahu, ceasefire

## tl;dr

France seeks to replicate late-2024 Lebanon ceasefire but faces deadlock; Israel has refused Paris’s mediation. Macron called for ceasefire in 5 March call with Netanyahu (first since France recognized Palestine; call friendly but brief and fruitless). By 8 March Israeli strikes had killed nearly 400 in Lebanon (83 children), displaced 500,000+. Paris also asked Iranian president to rein in Hezbollah. French diplomat: at this stage Israel has refused all French attempts. Lebanon ambassador to France: wounds so deep rebuilding will take time even if war ended today.
