# Gold prices fall as Iran war escalation sparks rally in oil, dollar

**Date:** 2026-03-08  
**Source:** Investing.com (Australia)  
**URL:** https://m.au.investing.com/news/commodities-news/gold-prices-fall-as-iran-war-escalation-sparks-rally-in-oil-dollar-4299551  
**Tags:** gold, oil, dollar, commodities, inflation

## tl;dr

Gold prices fell as escalation in the Iran war sparked a rally in oil and the US dollar. Higher oil lifts inflation expectations and supports the dollar, which typically weighs on gold; gold also came under pressure as a source of liquidity during sharp equity market declines.
