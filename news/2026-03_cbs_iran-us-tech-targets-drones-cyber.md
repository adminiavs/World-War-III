## tl;dr

Iran Tasnim (IRGC-linked) listed US tech companies as targets: Amazon, Microsoft, Palantir, Oracle. Iranian drone strikes have already damaged AWS data centres in two countries; Handala (Iran-linked) claimed cyberattack on Stryker (Microsoft environment). White House claimed Iranian ballistic attacks down 90 pct and drone attacks down 83 pct. Tasnim: Irans legitimate targets are gradually expanding to infrastructure and cyberwarfare.

**Impact on predictions:** Expands war into economic and digital infrastructure; fits asymmetric warfare and GCC as battleground.
