# As oil prices rise, Trump's allies fear: has Israel gone too far in Iran?

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** The Times
**Author:** David Charter
**URL:** https://www.thetimes.com/us/american-politics/article/gas-price-oil-barrel-iran-us-trump-israel-zw6bzptmw
**Tags:** graham, israel, oil, gasoline, trump, midterms

## tl;dr

Trump allies worry Israel is going too far bombing Iranian oil installations as petrol prices test voter support. Lindsey Graham (X): asked Israel to be cautious about targets; goal is to liberate Iranian people without crippling their chance for a better life—Iran’s oil economy will be essential. Israeli strikes on oil depots produced stark TV images; US gallon $3.48 Monday (up ~17% since attacks), highest of Trump presidency. Axios: White House adviser said president doesn’t like the attack, wants to save oil, doesn’t want to burn it—reminds people of higher gas prices. Maga base still with Trump; Hegseth on 60 Minutes: this is only just the beginning.
