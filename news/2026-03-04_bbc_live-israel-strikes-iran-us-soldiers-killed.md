## Full story

Live page. Summary of main points:

Israel says it is carrying out a fresh new wave of strikes in Iran, after the country earlier said it had carried out new attacks on Iran's "leadership compound" on Tuesday. BBC Verify assesses videos of the aftermath.

Meanwhile President Trump told reporters at the White House "everything's been knocked out in Iran". He also criticised Prime Minister Starmer and the UK's initial response to US-Israeli strikes on Iran, saying "this is not Winston Churchill we're dealing with".

The US says it has hit more than 2,000 targets in Iran since it began its military operations on Saturday. Tehran has retaliated with a wave of attacks across the region.

The number of people killed in Iran so far has reached 787, the Red Crescent says, while Tehran residents tell BBC Persian they are worried about the availability of groceries.

The UK will send helicopters with counter-drone capabilities and air defence destroyer HMS Dragon to Cyprus, after attacks on a British base there. France is planning to send a frigate and further air defences to the island too.

Notable live updates included: UK charters flight to take British nationals out of Oman; ship struck off coast of UAE (Fujairah); Israel issues more evacuation warnings in Lebanon (over a dozen villages); Asia markets continue to fall, oil edges higher; girl dies from falling shrapnel in Kuwait (11-year-old); HRANA says more than 1,000 civilians killed in Iran (181 children under 10), 5,402 injuries; Qatar says it arrested 10 suspected IRGC spies; funerals for victims of girls' school airstrike in Minab; Saudi Arabia says ready to respond to Iranian attacks; CIA station in Saudi Arabia hit in drone attack (US media); Iran operation "double" scale of 2003 shock and awe (CENTCOM: nearly 2,000 targets, 17 Iranian ships, no Iranian ship underway in Arabian Gulf/Strait of Hormuz/Gulf of Oman); Trump to see first test on war powers (Senate vote on War Powers Act); EU says not concerned about supply of oil and gas; Israeli ambassador to UN urges Lebanon to disarm Hezbollah; Israel says it struck approximately 60 Hezbollah targets; Israel and Hezbollah continue to exchange fire.
