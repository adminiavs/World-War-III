## tl;dr

Labor-aligned anti-war groups are circulating a draft motion calling on the Albanese government to reverse its support for the US-Israel war on Iran. The motion reflects grassroots and party-base pressure to align with the 2025 election repudiation of Trumpism and to distance Australia from the illegal conflict.
