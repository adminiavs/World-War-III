# Prewar US intel assessment: military intervention in Iran unlikely to change leadership

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Source:** Associated Press  
**URL:** https://apnews.com/article/iran-intelligence-assessment-trump-ad20c1f1168d4318af516d7b19d372e7

**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** US  
**Tags:** intelligence, nic, regime-change, assessment, trump, mojtaba-khamenei

## tl;dr

National Intelligence Council (NIC) assessment completed shortly before Feb 28 US-Israel strike concluded that neither limited airstrikes nor a larger, prolonged campaign would likely result in regime change — even if current leadership was killed. No unified opposition coalition poised to take over; Iran's establishment would attempt continuity. Assessment undercuts administration's claim of quick completion. Mojtaba Khamenei's selection as successor aligns with NIC findings. ODNI spokesperson declined to comment. Trump historically dismissive of intelligence community. Tulsi Gabbard (ODNI) fired NIC acting chairperson last year after declassified memo contradicted Venezuela deportation rationale.
