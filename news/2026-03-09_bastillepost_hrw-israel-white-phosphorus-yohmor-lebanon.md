# Human Rights Watch accuses Israel of using white phosphorus in southern Lebanese town

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** Bastille Post Global (AP)
**URL:** https://www.bastillepost.com/global/article/5673382-human-rights-watch-accuses-israel-of-using-white-phosphorus-in-southern-lebanese-town
**Tags:** lebanon, israel, white-phosphorus, hrw, yohmor, incendiary

## tl;dr

Human Rights Watch said in a report Monday that the Israeli military "unlawfully" hit the southern Lebanese village of Yohmor with shells containing white phosphorus. HRW geolocated and verified seven images showing Israel fired white phosphorus via artillery at residential areas, hours after the Israeli military warned Yohmor and dozens of other southern Lebanese villages to evacuate. HRW could not independently confirm whether residents were still present or anyone was harmed. Rights advocates say use of white phosphorus in populated areas is illegal under international law; it can set buildings on fire and burn human flesh to the bone; survivors risk infection and organ or respiratory failure. Ramzi Kaiss (HRW): "The Israeli military's unlawful use of white phosphorus over residential areas is extremely alarming and will have dire consequences for civilians." Israel has not immediately responded; historically it has said it uses white phosphorus as a smoke screen and not to target civilians. HRW and Amnesty documented use in Israel's last war with Hezbollah (over a year ago) on numerous occasions in southern Lebanon while civilians were present.
