# Turkiye says Iranian ballistic missile intercepted by NATO air defences

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** Al Jazeera

## tl;dr

Turkish Ministry of National Defence: NATO air defences intercepted a ballistic missile launched from Iran toward Turkiye. Intercepted Monday over Sahinbey district of **Gaziantep**, southern Turkiye. No casualties or damage. Second time an Iranian ballistic missile fired toward Turkiye since 28 Feb.
