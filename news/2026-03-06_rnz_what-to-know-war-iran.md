## Full story

The Detail podcast. Foreign correspondent Anna Fifield: 1953 US-UK coup against Mosaddegh (oil nationalisation), Shah restored, led to 1979 revolution and Islamic Republic. Iran, Israel and US as main players; Khamenei killed Saturday; Iran nuclear and proxy support; 12-day war June 2025; January protests and crackdown part of timing. NZ PM Luxon called for de-escalation, ruled out military involvement, focused on Kiwis in region; Newsroom associate editor Sachdeva said balancing condemnation with rules and law is where governments struggle. Fifield: wars rarely stay contained; oil, markets and spread would affect NZ. RNZ 6 Mar 2026, Amanda Gillies.
