# Iran allows two gas tankers to sail to India through Hormuz (sources)

**Date:** 13 March 2026
**Source:** Reuters
**URL:** https://www.reuters.com/business/energy/iran-allows-two-gas-tankers-sail-india-through-hormuz-sources-say-2026-03-13/

---

## tl;dr

Iran permitted two India-flagged LPG carriers (Pushpak and Parimal) to transit the Strait of Hormuz after diplomatic contact between Indian FM S. Jaishankar and Iranian counterpart Abbas Araghchi. A Liberia-flagged tanker carrying Saudi crude (Shenlong Suezmax) also reached Mumbai 10-12 March. India had about 28 Indian-flagged vessels stuck around the strait. Iranian ambassador to India said Tehran would provide safe passage to India-bound vessels. Iran is selectively allowing passage while blocking others.

**Impact on predictions:** Selective opening for India fits Iran using the Strait as leverage rather than total permanent closure.
