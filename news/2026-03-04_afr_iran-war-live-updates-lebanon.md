## Full story

Live updates page. The Middle East is entering its fifth day of war following the US and Israel's attack on Iran.

Iran continues to launch missiles and drones at targets across the Middle East in retaliation. Reports from Iranian non-state media claim that Ayatollah Ali Khamenei's second son, Mojtaba Khamenei, has been elected the new supreme leader. US President Donald Trump says the US Navy will be directed to escort tankers through the Strait of Hormuz. Israeli troops entered southern Lebanon as Hezbollah launched a cross-border attack. An Iranian drone attack targeted the US consulate in Dubai.

Key posts in the live feed: Chalmers says impact of war is uncertain but substantial; US military says attack is double 2003 shock and awe; Sydney-bound Emirates flight leaves Dubai; fire at super fund-backed oil hub in the UAE; Ayatollah's son reportedly elected new supreme leader; heavy bombardment in Tehran.
