# Iran war fuel crisis forces Pakistan to close schools, take austerity measures

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** Middle East Eye
**URL:** https://www.middleeasteye.net/news/iran-war-fuel-crisis-forces-pakistan-closes-schools-austerity-measures
**Tags:** pakistan, fuel, austerity, sharif

## tl;dr

PM Sharif: schools closed through end of month; half of workforce and university students remote. Government expenses cut 20%; cabinet no salary two months; MPs 25% wage cut; no foreign trips; official iftars banned. Pakistan depends on Gulf fuel; Hormuz and Gulf disruption hit supply. Sharif offered to mediate.
