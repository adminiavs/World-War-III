# Australia would be signed up to Iran war by deception and stealth, Shoebridge says

**Date:** 2026-03-09  
**Source:** The Guardian  
**URL:** https://www.theguardian.com/australia-news/2026/mar/09/australia-iran-war-deception-greens-shoebridge  
**Authors:** Tom McIlroy, Ben Doherty  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** Australia  
**Tags:** greens, shoebridge, military-support, international-law

## tl;dr

Greens defence spokesperson David Shoebridge: sending military support to Gulf would sign Australia up to Iran war "by deception and stealth." Donald Rothwell (ANU): assisting Gulf state self-defence makes Australia legally party to armed conflict, regardless of offensive/defensive distinction. Abbott: Australia doing nothing practical; "shameful." Wong: families of officials told to leave UAE. Australians for War Powers Reform: any troop commitment should be debated by parliament. Three Australian submariners on US sub that sank Dena. Two US P-8As at RAAF Pearce (purpose undisclosed). Shadow FM O'Brien: would "of course" be seen as participation. Labor considering support measures.
