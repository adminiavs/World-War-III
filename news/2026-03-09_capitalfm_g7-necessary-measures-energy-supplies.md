# G7 to take necessary measures to support energy supplies

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** Capital FM Kenya (BBC)
**URL:** https://www.capitalfm.co.ke/business/2026/03/g7-to-take-necessary-measures-to-support-energy-supplies/
**Tags:** g7, oil, iea, reserves, reeves

## tl;dr

G7 finance ministers and IEA met; ready to take necessary measures including stockpile release but no agreement to release yet. Oil touched $119.50 before falling to ~$102. Fatih Birol: markets deteriorated; Hormuz transit and curtailed production creating significant risks; IEA members hold 1.2bn barrels public plus 600m industry stocks. Reeves: UK called for immediate de-escalation and guaranteed ship security; ready to support coordinated IEA release. About a fifth of world oil usually through Hormuz; traffic all but halted. Trump dismissed oil concerns on Truth Social.
