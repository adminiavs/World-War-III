## Full story

Despite sensationalist reports of failures of modern Chinese air defense systems in Iran, multiple experts told Domino Theory there is no evidence they were used or even delivered. After the 12-day war in June 2025 when Israel and the US decimated Iranian air defenses, it was widely reported that Iran had taken delivery of the relatively advanced HQ-9B. But there are no pictures of the HQ-9B in Iranian service, no satellite images suggesting deployment, and nothing from US or Israeli governments that their forces have encountered it. Xu Tianran, a senior analyst on Northeast Asian security and missile systems, said: As far as I'm concerned, there's zero evidence that Iran operates any Chinese air defense missiles like the Hongqi, the HQ-9B. It's just not there. The HQ-9B is long-range, comparable to the Russian S-300 (which Iran operates). Middle East Eye reported Iran had received new air defense missiles from China in July 2025; Army Recognition said they were HQ-9Bs. The Chinese Embassy in Israel denied, stating China never exports weapons to countries engaged in warfare. Analysts cited strategic reasons China would not sell advanced weapons to Iran now: China gets more oil from the Arab Gulf than Iran; Joshua Arostegui (former US military intel, chair China Studies at US Army War College) said the foreign ministry would have gone into danger mode to get those denials out. Arostegui and Carl Rhodes (Robust Policy) noted that HQ-9Bs would be seen if in use (you don't keep them hidden; hidden they don't do any good) and that US/Israel would get signals intelligence when radars emit. Rhodes thinks it possible China or Chinese firms are assisting Iran with intelligence and satellite imagery; Houthis were getting imagery from Chang Guang Satellite Technology via the Iranian military. Both found late February reporting that Iran would buy the CM-302 anti-ship missile from China unlikely, and too late to have impact. Xu said that in Venezuela, Russian air defense systems were not moved for weeks before the American attack and were destroyed in place; there are no confirmed reports of Iran downing any US or Israeli crewed aircraft, so it is difficult to parse equipment quality versus how Iranian forces operate. Domino Theory 6 Mar 2026, Josh Hoang-Wilkes.
