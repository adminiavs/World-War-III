# US-owned tanker attacked near Iraq was hit by unmanned boats

**Date:** 13 March 2026
**Source:** Reuters
**URL:** https://www.reuters.com/world/middle-east/us-owned-tanker-attacked-near-iraq-was-hit-by-unmanned-boats-early-findings-show-2026-03-13/

## tl;dr

US-owned tanker Safesea Vishnu attacked in Iraqi waters by Iranian unmanned boats. At least one dead, 38 crew rescued. IRGC claimed responsibility. Six ships attacked in under 48 hours; 18+ vessels since 28 Feb. Early findings: unmanned boats used.

**Impact:** IRGC asymmetric naval tactics; Strait remains contested.
