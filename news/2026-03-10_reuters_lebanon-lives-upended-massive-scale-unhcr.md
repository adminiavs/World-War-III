# Lives being upended on massive scale in Lebanon, UN says

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Source:** Reuters  
**URL:** https://www.reuters.com/world/middle-east/lives-being-upended-massive-scale-lebanon-says-un-refugee-agency-2026-03-10/  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** Lebanon  
**Tags:** lebanon, unhcr, displacement, hezbollah, israel

## tl;dr

UN Refugee Agency: nearly 700,000 displaced in Lebanon in one week; 200,000+ children. More than 560 shelters opened. Israel-Hezbollah escalation with US-Israel Iran war. Southern Lebanon and Dahieh (Beirut) evacuation orders; humanitarian crisis. Syria reports 78,000+ Syrian nationals returning from Lebanon. UNICEF: protect civilians and civilian infrastructure.
