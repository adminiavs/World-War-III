# Dollar whipsawed on hopes of an end to the Iran war

**Date:** 2026-03-09
**Source:** Ynet (Reuters)
**URL:** https://www.ynetnews.com/business/article/r170pi3fzx
**Tags:** dollar, oil, trump, markets, brent

## tl;dr

Dollar pared gains after Trump told CBS the war was “very complete,” easing fears of prolonged conflict. Euro recovered from three-month low; dollar off six-week peak vs yen. Brent last ~$90.35 after earlier surge to $119.50. Analysts: market trading on hope; if war is over, dollar comes off and stocks rally—but outlook still murky. Weak US jobs data Friday had briefly stalled dollar; by Monday that had faded. Asia and euro zone heavily exposed to energy shock; prolonged conflict plus currency weakness would feed inflation. Bitcoin rose 3%.
