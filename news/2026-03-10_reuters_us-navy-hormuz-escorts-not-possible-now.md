# US Navy tells shipping industry Hormuz escorts not possible now

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Source:** Reuters  
**URL:** https://www.reuters.com/world/middle-east/us-navy-tells-shipping-industry-hormuz-escorts-not-possible-now-2026-03-10/  
**Content type:** news  
**Region/country:** Gulf / global  
**Tags:** hormuz, navy, escorts, shipping, tankers

## tl;dr

US Navy has informed the shipping industry that escorting tankers through the Strait of Hormuz is not feasible at this time, despite Trump's offer to send escorts and Energy Secretary Chris Wright's since-deleted claim that the Navy had begun escorting tankers. Over 1,000 vessels reportedly waiting to pass through; surplus too great for US assets to assist. Fox News (Jen Griffin): no US assets have escorted tankers.
