# Gas Prices Surge in U.S. as Iran War Chokes Global Oil Supply

**Date:** 2026-03  
**Source:** TIME  
**URL:** https://time.com/7383060/gas-prices-iran-war-oil/  
**Author:** Richard Hall  
**Tags:** gas-prices, oil, hormuz, trump, bessent, russian-oil-waiver

## tl;dr

National average for gallon of regular gasoline jumped 14% in a week to $3.41 Saturday; was under $3 a week ago. Conflict disrupted oil flows through Strait of Hormuz; crude above $90. Last similar weekly jump was March 2022 Russia/Ukraine. Hormuz effectively closed; about 20% of world crude and gas passes through. Iran Revolutionary Guard spokesman Saturday: Strait open to all except US and Israeli ships; we will target those. Tankers through strait dropped to zero since Wednesday. Trump: I don't have any concern; they'll drop very rapidly when this is over. Treasury Secretary Bessent issued 30-day waiver on US sanctions on Russian oil to India to increase supply. White House: Trump had strong game plan before Epic Fury; political risk insurance for cargo ships, Navy escorts, freeing sanctioned oil.
