## tl;dr

Australia has relaxed fuel standards to allow higher-sulphur (dirtier) fuel imports to protect supply security as the Iran war disrupts shipments through the Strait of Hormuz. Prices are surging at the pump. The move is part of the government's response to the fuel supply crunch; see also day13 (ABC): Energy Minister Chris Bowen approved higher-sulphur fuel to be blended into domestic supply for 60 days, adding an estimated 100 million litres monthly.
