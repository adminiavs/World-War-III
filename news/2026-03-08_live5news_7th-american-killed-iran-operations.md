## tl;dr

U.S. military announced an American service member has died of injuries sustained during an Iranian attack on troops stationed in Saudi Arabia. The seventh American service member killed in the Iran war was an Army soldier. The service member died on Saturday from injuries sustained during a March 1 Iranian attack on the kingdom. American death toll since the start of combat operations now stands at seven. Six Army reservists were killed in the March 1 attack on Kuwait (Port Shuaiba). All seven casualties from the war in Iran were Army soldiers.
