# CNN poll: 59% disapprove of Iran strikes; most think long-term conflict likely

**Date:** 2026-03-02
**Source:** CNN / Reuters Ipsos / POLITICO (consolidated)
**URL:** https://www.cnn.com/2026/03/02/politics/cnn-poll-59-of-americans-disapprove-of-iran-strikes-and-most-think-a-long-term-conflict-is-likely
**Tags:** polls, trump, approval, domestic, congress

## tl;dr

Early March 2026: CNN 59% disapprove, 41% approve; NBC 54% disapprove of Trump's handling; Reuters/Ipsos 27% approve, 43% disapprove; WaPo/SSRS 52% oppose, 39% support. 60% say Trump lacks clear plan; 62% want congressional approval for further action; 56% long-term conflict likely; 54% say strikes will make Iran more of a threat; 56% say Trump too willing to use military force. 42% of Republicans less likely to support if US casualties. Partisan split: ~80% MAGA Republicans support; majority overall oppose.
