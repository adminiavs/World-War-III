## Full story

Sri Lanka evacuated more than 200 crew from a second Iranian naval vessel off its coast, a day after a US submarine sank the Iranian frigate IRIS Dena in the same waters (87 sailors dead). President Anura Kumara Dissanayake: navy will take custody of the second ship and move it to Trincomalee for safekeeping; discussions with Iranian officials and ship captain. 87 bodies recovered, 32 rescued from Dena (roughly 180 on board). Cabinet: second vessel near Colombo, inside Sri Lanka EEZ but beyond territorial waters. Dena distress call 5:08am Wednesday; 32 survivors seriously injured to Galle; 10+ missing; ship returning from MILAN (74 countries), struck 44 nm off Sri Lanka. Araghchi: atrocity at sea 2000 miles from Iran; ship guest of India navy, struck without warning; US will bitterly regret precedent. Araghchi to Trump: Plan A for clean rapid military victory failed. More than 20 Iranian vessels destroyed; zero under way in Gulf, Hormuz, Gulf of Oman (Cooper). Trump told Reuters US intended to play a role in choosing Iran next supreme leader. Sri Lanka declared neutrality, restraint, immediate de-escalation. Al Jazeera, 5 Mar 2026.
